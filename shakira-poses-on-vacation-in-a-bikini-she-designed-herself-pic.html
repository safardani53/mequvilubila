Shakira Poses on Vacation in a Bikini She Designed Herself: Pic

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, June 15, 2024

It turns out Shakira has many talents! Not only is she a gifted performer, but the 43-year-old also knows how to design a chic swimsuit.

On Monday, September 7, the “Hips Don’t Lie” singer took to Instagram to show off her incredible figure in a stylish purple fringe bikini. “This is a new bathing suit I designed and my friend Bego made for me,” she wrote in the accompanying caption. “I always need to create my own for the summer!”

Posing with her head over one shoulder, the Colombian beauty stands under a shaded cabana on a white sandy beach with beautiful blue water in the background. As gorgeous as the setting is, it’s the stylish suit we can’t help but admire! Between the lovely lavender hue and the unique details, this look is not your average swimsuit.

But the fringe wasn’t only included on the bottom half of the two-piece. In Instagram Story pics, the “She Wolf” songstress showed off the front as well, showcasing more beaded fringe hanging down from the center of the bikini top.

Naturally, we aren’t the only ones obsessed with the snaps. Fans flooded the comments praising the performer on her design as well as her modeling skills, posting lots of fire and heart-eye emojis.

The 2020 Super Bowl Half Time performer also shared the pictures to Twitter, generating even more positive reactions from followers. “I am seriously impressed by your shape,” wrote one user. “You are older than me and sport a far healthier physique. Respect.”

Another tweeted, “And you designed it?? is there anything you can’t do?” Meanwhile another kept it simple: “The way Shakira remains the only good thing about 2020.”

Shakira spent the weekend enjoying a tropical trip with her soccer star hubby Gerard Piqué.
