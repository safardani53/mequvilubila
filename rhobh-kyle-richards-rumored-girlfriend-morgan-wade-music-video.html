RHOBH's Kyle Richards locks lips with rumored girlfriend Morgan Wade in steamy love scene for singer

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

RHOBH star Kyle Richards has left fans stunned, locking lips with her rumored girlfriend Morgan Wade in a steamy music video scene.

The Virginia-born country singer released the video on Thursday, getting Bravo fans buzzing.

In the video, Kyle, 54, is seen pining after Morgan, 29, as steamy lyrics about desire play in the music video.

The RHOBH star is seen drafting a love letter to send to the singer, who has seemingly hatched a plan of her own to win her over.

Another scene showed the duo in a bathtub together blowing bubbles.

Late in the clip, Kyle is seen watering her garden while Morgan washes her car.

The pair repeatedly make eyes at one another before Morgan finally approaches, stealing a kiss from the Bravo reality star.

In another scene, Kyle is seen wearing lingerie while she and Morgan sensually feed fruit and whipped cream to one another and share yet another kiss.

Rumors have been swirling that the Halloween star and musician are a couple, with fans noticing how much time they've been spending together as of late.

They denied being a couple but that hasn't stopped speculation.

SETTING IT STRAIGHT

Eagle-eyed fans have picked up on a number of clues, including the fact that Morgan has a "K" tattoo.

Additionally, she and Kyle appear to have matching small tattoos.

Kyle has denied that she is seeing anyone else since she and her husband Mauricio Umansky announced their decision to take a break earlier this summer.

She previously told a paparazzo that she is "not single," leading some supporters to believe that her marriage to Mauricio is far from over.

Morgan also did her part to shut down the speculation.

Days before the release of the Make You Fall In Love With Me video, she and Kyle appeared in behind-the-scenes footage from the making of the video.

They addressed their relationship, revealing how they became friends.

Morgan also shut down speculation that they're more than friends, saying: "I've done several music videos and every time someone is in it with me, they assume we're dating or something is going on.

"The internet is obsessed and why I'd be friends with you."

She went on: "We thought it would kind of be a good idea to poke fun at that and troll the trolls a little bit. The Internet is going to be popping off about this one, I'm sure."

Kyle then chimed in: "Well, if they're gonna talk, we might as well give them something to talk about."

SHOCKING SPLIT

RHOBH fans are eager to get all the details about Kyle and Mauricio's shocking separation and it looks like they'll get them on the upcoming season of the show.

The couple announced their relationship troubles shortly after filming wrapped, leading Bravo's production crew to pick the cameras back up and begin filming again to capture the aftermath.

Sources close to the show told TMZ that the crew went back to film with some members of the cast after the couple announced they were taking time apart.

It's unclear how long filming resumed.

The insider told TMZ that Bravo was not expecting a long shoot schedule.

The main goal of filming was to capture the reaction to the split bombshell.

RHOBH finished filming in May but producers felt it was necessary to resume filming.

Kyle and Mauricio's apparent separation news broke on July 4.

An insider told People: "Kyle and Mauricio have been separated for a while now but are still living under the same roof. They remain amicable as they figure out what’s next for them and their family."

The source told the outlet that they intend to continue to live together.

The couple met at a nightclub in 1994 then two years later, the two married in January 1996.

Kyle and Mauricio share three daughters: Alexia, 27; Sophia, 23; and Portia, 15.

The RHOBH star also has another daughter, Farrah, 34, from a previous marriage.
