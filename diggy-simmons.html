Diggy Simmons Biography

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, April 9, 2024

Diggy is now aged 27 years old and his height is five feet and nine inches tall. He loves to spend his spare time with his family and friends. There are no rumors about his love story. His nationality is American and he is of black ethnicity.

Diggy has been active as a rapper since 2009. His debut mixtape The First Flight was released in 2009 and he released it in his own blog. This mixtape was downloaded by more than 100,000 people. After his popularity and fame, several recording companies showed their interest in signing them but he chose Atlantic Records.

In 2010, he released the cover of Made You Look and it went viral on the internet. His third mixtape Past Presents Future was released at the end of 2011. His debut album Unexpected Arrival was released on the 20th of March 2012. He has also been acting since 2012. His net worth is $3.75 million.
