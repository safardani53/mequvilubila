How many children does Christina Applegate have?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, April 25, 2024

ACTRESS Christina Applegate has been married to musician Martyn LeNoble since 2013.

The pair got engaged on Valentine’s Day of 2010, three years after her divorce from Johnathon Schaech.

How many children does Christina Applegate have?

Christina Applegate is a mom to Sadie Grace LeNoble, born in 2011.

The actress had announced her pregnancy back in July of 2010, and at the time dished to People about being a parent, saying: “I’ll probably be a little bit hippie and a little bit Type-A. 

“I’ll take from what my mother did, which was way hippie and like, ‘Do what you wanna do,’ and bring some things that I know from watching my friends raise their kids.

Adding: “An amalgamation of sorts.”

Applegate became pregnant after undergoing a double mastectomy due to a breast cancer diagnosis in 2008.

The cancer was only located on her left breast and was caught at an early stage.

She spoke to CNN about her procedure, saying: “It came on really fast. It was one of those things that I woke up and it felt so right. 

"It just seemed like, 'I don't want to have to deal with this again. I don't want to keep putting that stuff in my body. I just want to be done with this.' & I was just going to let them go."

Who is Christina Applegate's husband Martyn LeNoble?

After her four-year marriage with fellow actor Johnathon Schaech ended in a divorce, Applegate began dating Dutch musician Martyn LeNoble.

LeNoble is a Dutch bassist and a founding member of the alternative rock band Porno for Pyros.

The 52-year-old started his musical career by playing bass in a Dutch punk rock band when he was 14.

In 1989, he moved to Los Angeles and played with Thelonious Monster and the Too Free Stooges.

He joined Peter DiStefano, Stephen Perkins, and Perry Farrell to form Porno for Pyros three years later.

LeNoble has worked, recorded, toured, and performed with artists, including Jane's Addiction, the Cult, Dave Gahan, Scott Weiland, Sara McLachlan, Tom Morello, and Josh Klinghoffer, among others.
