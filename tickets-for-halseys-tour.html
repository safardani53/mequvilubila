Love and Power Tour: How can I buy tickets for Halseys tour?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, March 17, 2024

HALSEY will embark on a national tour later this year for their latest album, If I Can’t Have Love, I Want Power. 

Halsey’s fourth studio album is also available as a film set to the singer’s music, produced by HBO.

Love and Power Tour: How can I buy tickets for Halsey’s tour?

According to the tour’s website, all of the venues are outdoors and compliant with COVID-19 protocols.

Tickets for a select few of the venues are currently available, while pre-sale for the majority of the concerts starts February 1, 2022. 

Tickets can be purchased via Ticketmaster, LiveNation or directly from the concert venue.

Who is Halsey?

Halsey is a singer and musician from Edison, New Jersey.

Halsey burst onto the scene when they collaborated with The Chainsmokers for their song “Closer”.

The song received numerous awards after making rounds on radio stations as a summer anthem in 2017. 

Now a reputable singer and songwriter, Halsey has collaborated with Machine Gun Kelly, ex-partner YUNGBLUD, Future, and more. 

Halsey and her partner Alev Aydin welcomed a son in July 2021.
