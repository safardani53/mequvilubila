Spooky season is in full swing, so it’s time for some pumpkin patch fun. See stars gearing up for Halloween at the best pumpkin patches.

The leaves are falling, the weather is chilly, and we can smell the pumpkin spice lattes. Fall is officially here and we know one of the best activities for the crisp, autumn season is a trip to the pumpkin patch.

If you think you’re the only one who enjoys a day among the orange decorations, think again! Celebrities love bringing their closest kin to the pumpkin patch. In fact, they can’t wait for the moment to pick the best pumpkin for their jack-o-lantern, doorstep decoration, or interior accessory!

A slew of Hollywood stars’ go-to spot is Mr. Bones Pumpkin Patch. Whether it’s on a date, out with friends, or making memories with the kids, you can’t really beat the good times that a pumpkin patch brings. So warm up some apple cider and take a look at what the celebs were up to at their favorite pumpkin patch!
