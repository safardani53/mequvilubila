See Photos Of The Glowing Looks Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, October 8, 2024

Over the years, plenty of stars have stepped out without wearing makeup, allowing a fresh-faced glow that we don’t often see. Jennifer Lopez is another star who loves to go makeup-free. Whether she’s running to the gym or just showing off her naturally gorgeous face on social media, she pulls off the no-makeup look perfectly. JLo recently posted a makeup-free video to celebrate her 54th birthday and she looked just as good without makeup as she does without.

Kim Kardashian knows how to dress up for a red carpet like the best of them, but she’s also a mom of four, so she’s certainly been photographed running out of the house without makeup, too. Kim showed off her naturally accentuated cheekbones while wearing not a stitch of makeup on her face while holding her sister, Khloe Kardashian’s son, Tatum.

Kim’s not the only celebrity over 40 to look fabulous without makeup, as Jessica Simpson absolutely glowed in an Instagram selfie taken when she got out of the shower.

Kim’s sister, Kendall Jenner, often goes for an understated makeup look and shows off her natural beauty, so she’s pulled this off quite a number of times. Other models, like Gigi Hadid, Bella Hadid, and Kaia Gerber, have also rocked the no-makeup look. Even Kylie Jenner – the makeup QUEEN – has been photographed out without her face done up and of course, she still looked fabulous!

Click through the gallery above to check out these stars and more – including Hailey Bieber and others – who have shown off their makeup-free looks. Of course, we love seeing them get glammed up, too, but everyone needs to take off the filter and just go au naturale every once in a while!
