The Lying Life of Adults Is Set To Be Released On Netflix

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, October 3, 2024

The Lying Life of Adults, Netflix’s next Italian-language drama series, will premiere on Wednesday, January 4, 2023, at 3 a.m. ET. The story follows heroine Giovanna as she discovers her inner self while exploring the lovely historical city of Naples. The drama stars Giordana Marengo in the major role, as well as a slew of other performers in key supporting parts. Edoardo De Angelis is the director of The Lying Life of Adults.

Giovanna embarks on a fascinating journey of self-discovery in Netflix’s The Lying Life of Adults.

On December 20, 2022, Netflix published the official trailer for The Lying Life of Adults, which gives a glimpse of heroine Giovanna’s interesting adolescent life. It starts with a voiceover that beautifully sets the tone for the rest of the show:

“When you’re little, everything seems enormous. Everything seems little when you’re an adult.”

The teaser then depicts various exciting sequences from the program without divulging important facts that might possibly ruin viewers’ watching experience. Overall, the clip has a positive tone that lovers of coming-of-age dramas will undoubtedly like. Along with the teaser, Netflix released an official series synopsis, which reads:

“When you’re a kid, everything appears huge. When you’re large, everything appears little.” A phrase that sums up Giovanna’s story: “her passage from childhood, when every obstacle seemed insurmountable, to maturity, revealing her family’s falsehoods while finding an unparalleled Naples.”

The summary continues:

“In 1990s Naples, outspoken and daring Aunt Vittoria takes her sheltered niece on a tour of the city, shocking the teen’s conservative parents.”

According to the narrative and teaser, viewers can expect a nostalgic and emotional journey back to the 1990s with various intriguing and distinct individuals. The music and visuals shown in the trailer reflect the iconic 90s atmosphere. The series will supposedly include six episodes, all of which will be broadcast on January 4, 2023.

Meet Valeria Golino and the rest of the cast.

Giovanna is portrayed by Giordana Marengo. She appears extremely good in her job, as she represents her character’s different nuanced colors with breathtaking ease and confidence. Viewers should anticipate a strong performance from her on her debut. Valeria Golino, who plays Giovanna’s vivacious and brave Aunt Vittoria, co-stars with Marengo in another pivotal part. Golino looks fantastic in the series teaser, effortlessly embodying the elements that distinguish her character.

Alessandro Preziosi, Pina Turco, Adriano Pantaleo, and many more round out the supporting cast. The Vice of Hope, Mozzarella Stories, and many more films have been directed by Edoardo De Angelis. Remember to watch The Lying Life of Adults on Netflix on January 4, 2023.
