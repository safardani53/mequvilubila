Conor McGregor wears customised suit which has 'f*** you' written in pinstripes for Floyd Mayweather

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 4, 2024

CONOR MCGREGOR was looking sharp in his customised suit as he came face to face with Floyd Mayweather in Los Angeles.

The MMA superstar was wearing a suit from his new line of fashion he's released.

But the one he had on had an extra special message hidden in the detail that was hard to spot with the naked eye.

Fortunately, McGregor bragged about the fact his pinstripes read "f*** you".

His loud suit matched his brash performance at the first of four press conferences this week to promote the fight against Mayweather on August 26.

McGregor had a laugh at Mayweather's expense, saying the American boxer "can't afford a suit anymore" in a jibe towards his well documented tax issues.

The 28-year-old continued: “He is f***ed – there is no other way about it.

“His little legs, his little core, his little head… I’m going to knock him out in four rounds.

“He will be unconscious in four rounds. The movement power, ferociousness, he’s not experienced this.

“This limited set of skills isn’t even a true fight. If this was a real fight it would be one round."

Mayweather was unfazed by the UFC star's taunts.

He said: "I still got it.

“Twenty one is the number. That’s how long I’ve been kicking a**.

“I don’t give a f*** if it’s a ring or an octagon, put me in there and I’m going to kick a**.

“You line them up, I knock them out like bowling pins. On August 26 I’m going to knock this b**** out too.”
