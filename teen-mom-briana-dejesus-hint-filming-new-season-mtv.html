Teen Mom Briana DeJesus drops big hint she's already filming new season of MTV show with big clue in

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

TEEN Mom star Briana DeJesus has hinted that she's already working on a new season of the MTV reality show.

The network just debuted a new season of Teen Mom: Family Reunion, on which Briana and her mom Roxanne are both appearing.

Briana's sister Brittany took to her Instagram Stories on Thursday to reveal that the entire family – which consists of her sister, mom Roxanne, and nieces Nova and Stella – are back to work with the network.

Just a few short weeks after the last season of Teen Mom: The Next Chapter came to an end.

She shared a video that showed herself and Briana, 28, sitting on a sectional sofa.

As the phone panned around the room, a camera person could be seen with equipment pointed at the sibling duo.

Brittany and Briana appeared to be deep in conversation as two small dogs played nearby.

The MTV star captioned the post: "My mother's dogs be doing too much!"

Her caption suggests that they were filming at Roxanne's house, which both sisters moved out of during the last season of Teen Mom.

Briana bought a home for herself and her daughters, which her then-boyfriend Bobby – a former MTV security staffer – helped her move into.

Then, on Christmas day, she surprised her daughters with a new home.

BRIANA'S PAIN

Briana's sister isn't currently appearing on Teen Mom: Family Reunion, but her mom and sister are.

During the premiere of the second season earlier this week, the cast went bungee jumping as part of an exercise in letting go of fear.

Briana agreed to jump, though she was nervous.

Before the jump, each of the Teen Mom cast members stepped forward to reveal a fear that they were releasing.

The mom-of-two confessed: "I'm just tired."

She went on to elaborate: "I don't want to be everybody's person."

The mom of two claimed that she's had to be the "backbone" of her entire family, including kids Stella, Nova, and mom Roxanne. 

She said on the show that she has always put everyone before herself and feels that she's lost her sense of self. 

The reality star revealed: “Because I got pregnant so early, I feel like I don’t know who I am. I put myself  on the back burner to make sure my kids are ok, my mom is ok.”

She noted that it's "too much" for her to continue doing. 

Briana shared her thoughts as she got strapped into a harness and her mom Roxanne, tagging along for the MTV series, stepped away from the group.

“It pains me to hear my daughter Briana say that," she confessed. 

Her daughter, however, was confused by her distance.

She said in a confessional: “I’m the backbone for everybody in the family and it’s a lot of the pressure … I need my mom right now and she’s walking away.”

She went on: “Is she about to leave and not watch me jump off this bridge? I definitely need my mom right now and she just walked off.”

The pair finally came together before Briana's jump, with Roxanne explaining through tears: “It freaks me out. My anxiety. I can’t.”

She noted, "That is so far down," and added, "accidents happen."

In the end, however, she decided to put her fear aside and be there for Briana. 

Coach B, who was leading the experience and will continue to guide the retreat, assured Briana: “Your mom is saying ‘I got your back.’”

At that point, she started to break down.

Briana patted tears from her eyes as she got ready to take the plunge. 

Afterward, she admitted: “Jumping off that bridge released so much pressure for me.”

DRAMA INCOMING

While the first episode of the show packed an emotional punch, Briana has teased big drama ahead.

She claimed the rest of the season is a "hot mess express."

It was previously revealed that production was halted after she and co-star Ashley Jones got into a heated fight.

Briana teased the drama incoming as Teen Mom: The Next Chapter came to a close.

She tweeted at the time: “Season finale coming up only means Teen Mom: Family Reunion will start soon.”

She warned her fans: “And that s**t was a hot mess express,” adding two laughing emojis

Although the cast for the new season has yet to be announced, fans are already bracing themselves for drama.

The U.S. Sun previously reported how a fight broke out between Briana and Ashley, 25, and their mothers, Roxanne and Tea Chapple, during the filming of Family Reunion in August.

According to The Ashley’s Reality Roundup, the fight lasted several days and resulted in the production crew going on strike.

Per the outlet, Ashley spat on her cast member during a bitter disagreement while filming.

Members of the camera crew and sound technicians subsequently put down their equipment at the resort in Oregon where the spin-off was being filmed. 

The fight began during one of the first cast meals, a source told The Ashley’s Reality Roundup.

The cast was allegedly having a conversation about supporting one another on social media when things got ugly. 

"Roxanne made a comment about how the grandmas from the show need to be supportive of each other and [Tea] took it as a jab at her," an insider said. 

"She told Roxanne to say what she wanted to say, so Roxanne started talking about some of the stuff Tea has said about Briana on social media."

The source added: "They started screaming and cussing at each other and tried to get at each other but it was broken up by security."

However, that wasn't the end of the drama.

Several sources alleged that things got even more intense later that evening inside the resort.
