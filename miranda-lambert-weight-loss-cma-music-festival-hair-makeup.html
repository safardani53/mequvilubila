Miranda Lambert’s Weight Loss — Shows Off Body At CMA Music Festival

Miranda rocked her platinum blonde hair in a deep side part and beachy waves during the show. She wore a smokey eye look with a nude lip. Her overall look was so sexy!

Not only did she look amazing, but she sounded amazing.

Along with her hair and makeup, of course, we were loving her slim body.

Miranda Lambert’s Diet Tips — Tricks To Stay Slim

Miranda admits it’s all about balance and an overall healthy lifestyle.

“It’s really easy to gain weight and it’s really hard to lose weight, so just staying focused, and through the holidays not getting off track, you know, I had to really stay focused. If I ate bad in the morning, I tried to do better at night, or vice versa.

Just not blowing it all day long, everyday,” Miranda told the June 2014 issue of Women’s Health. 

Miranda does admit to indulging here and there: “My guilty pleasure is, well, some people would say it could be music or whatever, but mine is definitely food, and it’s mostly Cheetos.”
