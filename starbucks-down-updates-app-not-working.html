Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 10, 2024

Interesting tip for more rewards, continued

Starbucks said that only some customers can get the rewards.

The company said it is also valid "at participating stores on in-store beverage purchases only."

Customers may not be able to benefit while making a mobile order, as the reusable cup will be used to make the drink.

The Starbucks spokesperson said that reusable discounts for out-of-store orders will launch next year.

"At the start of next year (2024), customers will be able to bring their own clean, personal reusable cup for every store visit in the US and Canada – including in café, drive-thru and mobile order and pay," the spokesperson confirmed.

Interesting tip for more rewards

Starbucks fans have been saving cash and racking points with one quick and easy tip that makes them feel eco-conscious.

The company gives rewards and discounts to participating customers, but customers wave goodbye to some mobile-order benefits.

Customers can get a reward of 25 Stars and 10 cents off their drink order if they bring in a reusable cup.

A Starbucks spokesperson specified to The U.S. Sun that customers must bring a clean, reusable cup to get the benefits.

Using reusable cups is more eco-friendly than purchasing a drink in regular plastic cups.

Recent Starbucks closings

Starbucks fans have been left in a state of shock as the coffee giant shuts down five locations in one state.

The store closures are disrupting many coffee lovers’ routines in Connecticut, but the good news is that the shutdowns are only temporary.

Starbucks said the locations will be closed as the buildings undergo renovations, but each unit should reopen in just a few weeks.

So far, the temporary closures affect the following stores:

  • 995 Main Street, Newington
  • 538 Boston Post Road, Orange
  • 1030 Barnum Avenue, Stratford
  • 1090 Silas Deane Highway, Wethersfield
