Celebrity Big Brother stars in shock as EIGHT housemates up for eviction

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, July 7, 2024

CELEBRITY Big Brother housemates were left in shock this evening as it was revealed that EIGHT housemates were put up for eviction.

It was revealed that all housemates who received at least one nomination would be up for eviction.

The only people who did not receive any nominations were Calum Best and Bianca Gascoigne. James Cosmo could not be nominated.

Spencer and Heidi Pratt nominated Colleen Nolan and Nicola McLean, saying that the latter "broke what was left of our hearts".

Colleen nominated Kim Woodburn who she said became "Snow White" and she "doesn't get it". She also chose Jedward: "They're sweet but they're so bizarre and you can't have a proper conversation."

James nominated Kim, who she described as "rather condescending", and Jedward for their "excessive food consumption and continuous performance".

Jedward nominated Stacy ("there's only so much screaming you can hear") and Nicola, who they said was "always trying to start fights" with them.

Meanwhile Nicola voted for Kim, describing her as "fake" and Jedward because they cause her a lot of stress in the house and make her feel comfortable.

Jamie O'Hara nominated Kim simply because he "doesn't like her" and for Jessica because he "feels awkward around her".

Jessica Cunningham nominated Stacy and Jamie while Calum voted for Kim and Jedward because "they're super defensive" and "sneaky".
