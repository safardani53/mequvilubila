I live off grid &amp; rent free in a house I built myself - Im now slow living but the neighbours are

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, April 5, 2024

A WOMAN who built her own cabin from scratch to live off grid in the woods has given an insight into her unusual lifestyle.

Zorya, from the United States, even revealed how she deals with visits from bears on a day-to-day basis.

The TikToker describes her lifestyle as "slow living" as she immerses herself with nature and embraces the outdoor world.

And in a 17 second video uploaded onto the social media platform, Zorya shows viewers just why she chose to move to the woods.

Attached to the video is the caption: "POV: You moved to the woods to live your dream life."

Included are a selection of clips showing the stunning scenery and Zorya's newly adopted way of life.

Viewers can see how she cooks and what she eats, as well as what she forages for her meals.

There are also plenty of close encounters with nature, including squirrels, deer and even bears that come to visit her garden.

Zorya also shows how she collects wood for a fire and enjoys wandering through and exploring the forest.

Followers of the TikToker (._zorya._) were quick to make their way to the comments section of the video and express their thoughts on Zorya's iconic lifestyle.
