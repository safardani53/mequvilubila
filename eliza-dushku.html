Eliza Dushku's early life

Eliza Dushku was born on 30th December 1980 in Watertown, Massachusetts, the United States to Judith Dushku, a political science professor, and Philip R. Dushku, a teacher, and administrator. Her father is Albanian and her mother is an American.
