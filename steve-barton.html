Who is Steve Barton:

Steve Barton is a famous Stage Actor. He was born on June 26, 1954 and his birthplace is Arkansas. Steve is also well known as, Late stage actor who won critical acclaim for roles like Raoul in Phantom of the Opera and the Beast in Beauty and the Beast. He sadly committed suicide in Bremen, Germany at age forty-seven.

Steve is originated from United States. He played the role of Graf von Krolock in director Roman Polanski’s Tanz der Vampire.
