Sharon &amp; Ozzy Osbournes Cheating History Their Past Mistakes Revealed Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, May 25, 2024

Cheating is terrible, and we feel horrible that that may have been what ended Sharon’s 33-year relationship with Ozzy. However, we were surprised to find that the duo’s relationship was actually born out of an affair.

Ozzy was married to Thelma Riley from 1971-1982, who was by his side throughout his entire rise to fame in the 70’s with Black Sabbath, and after he went solo in 1979. Ozzy had had two children, Jessica and Louis with Thelma, and even adopted her son from a previous relationship. However, he ended his 11 year marriage with an affair with none other than Sharon herself, who he began an affair with in 1982.

That wasn’t the couple’s only run-in with infidelity. On Dec. 2003, Sharon released a tell-all book and went on Howard Stern to explain her cheating past. She shockingly revealed that she had gotten drunk and had sex with the guitarist for Ozzy’s band, Randy Rhoads at the very beginning of their relationship before they were married. Yikes! Luckily, Ozzy got over it, and he married Sharon the same year he divorced Thelma. What a sticky past!
