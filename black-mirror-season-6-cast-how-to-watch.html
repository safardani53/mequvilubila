Who is in the cast of Black Mirror season 6?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, February 27, 2024

BLACK Mirror fans are THRILLED the show is back for more dystopian madness and occurrences.

The long-running Netflix series is now in its sixth season and once again includes some very famous faces.

Who is in the cast of Black Mirror season 6?

Fans have been waiting for Black Mirror 6 for four years, after the second half of season five was released in 2019.

But now the hit series is back, and the star line-up is better than ever before.

Charlie Brooker's dark, satirical anthology series once again reinvents itself with each new episode.

Speaking about the return of Black Mirror and his star cast, showrunner Charlie told Netflix: "Bringing it all to life, we’ve got an incredible roster of disgustingly skillful, smart directors, working with a cast of actors so talented they frankly have no right to exist."

Aaron Paul

Emmy Award winner Aaron Paul is known for playing Jesse in Breaking Bad.

The talented star has also appeared in Westworld, and The Path.

Aaron will make his Black Mirror debut in the Beyond The Sea episode.

Viewers will watch an alternative 1969, where two men on a perilous high-tech mission wrestle with the consequences of an unimaginable tragedy.

Salma Hayek

Salma Hayek is a well known established Hollywood actress and Oscar nominee.

Audiences know the actress from House Of Gucci, The Eternals, and 30 Rock.

Salma features in the Black Mirror episode called Joan Is Awful, in which an average woman is stunned to discover a global streaming platform has launched a prestige TV drama adaptation of her life, where she is played by Salma Hayek.

Josh Hartnett

Josh Hartnett is a veteran actor who shot to fame in crime drama series Cracker, where he played the role of Michael Fitzgerald in 1997.

In 1998, he made his film debut in the movie, Halloween H20: 20 Years Later, which was followed by hit movie Pearl Harbor.

Josh also features in the Black Mirror episode called Beyond The Sea.

Kate Mara

Kate Mara is best known for playing Zoe Barnes in House Of Cards.

But she also received high praise when she starred in miniseries A Teacher.

Kate joins actors Aaron and Josh in the Beyond The Sea episode.

Michael Cera

Michael Cera shot to fame when he starred in TV show Arrested Development.

But he hit the big time when he appeared in critically acclaimed movie Juno.

Michael stars alongside Selma in the Joan Is Awful episode.

The rest of the Black Mirror season six cast includes:

  • Zazie Beetz
  • Anjana Vasan
  • Annie Murphy
  • Auden Thornton
  • Ben Barnes
  • Clara Rugaard
  • Daniel Portman
  • Danny Ramirez
  • David Shields
  • Himesh Patel
  • John Hannah
  • Monica Dolan
  • Myha’la Herrold
  • Paapa Essiedu
  • Rob Delaney
  • Rory Culkin
  • Samuel Blenkin

How can I watch Black Mirror season 6?

Black Mirror 6 is available to watch on Netflix from June 15, 2023.

The series will be available to stream in the US from 3am ET, while in the UK it drops at 8am.

You can watch seasons one to five on Netflix right now.
