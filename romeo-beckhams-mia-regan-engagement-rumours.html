Romeo Beckham's girlfriend Mia Regan stuns in tiny bikini as he cuddles her from behind after engage

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 17, 2024

MIA REGAN has stunned in a tiny bra as she cuddled boyfriend Romeo Beckham on the beach.

The showbiz pair have been dating for three years after first meeting in September 2019.

The loved-up couple, both 20, were enjoying a trip to the beach when they took the snap.

Romeo, son of footballing icon David and Victoria Beckham, can be seen cuddling his model girlfriend.

He had plenty of style, as he wore a Puma sports top while Mia stole the show in her stripped bra.

Romeo shared the picture with his 3.6million followers on Instagram.

Romeo had sparked engagement rumours when pictured with his model girlfriend as she flashed a huge diamond ring.

The outing to Soho House White City saw Mia attempt to hide her ring from photographers.

A source close to the Beckhams said it's not true, and Romeo isn't engaged when approached by The Sun.

Romeo has made his own way in football having started his career at Inter Miami.

In January, he joined Brentford B on loan and picked up the first trophy of his career earlier this month.

The club won the Premier League Cup, although he was an unused substitute in the 2-1 final victory over Blackburn.

After the game, the midfielder received his winner's medal and posed with his team-mates as they lifted the trophy.

Brentford are reportedly keen to extend his stay beyond this season.

Bees chiefs have insisted the signing of Romeo is far from a marketing move and that he has impressed coaches in West London.

Director of football Phil Giles told The High Performance Podcast: "It wasn't a PR stunt in any way.

"The thought that went into Romeo is that we have relationships in football, he was looking for places to train in England as he's an Inter Miami player.
