Amateur Career 

After graduating from Great Falls High School in Great Falls, South Carolina, Craig attended the University of South Carolina Upstate. In addition, he was named 1A State Player of the Year while in his senior year.

Craig entered the starting lineup as a freshman and quickly made an impact. He averaged 14.4 points and 7.2 rebounds per game on his way to being named Atlantic Sun Conference Freshman of the Year.

As a sophomore, Craig led the A-Sun averaging 16.4 points per game while helping the Spartans win 21 games.

The Associated Press named Craig the conference Player of the Year and an honorable mention All-American. As a junior, Craig led the A-Sun averaging 17.2 points per game. He was named to the Atlantic Sun’s first team.

Craig was named Atlantic Sun Preseason Player of the Year prior to the start of his senior season. Craig’s 2,000th career point came on February 10, 2014, when he scored 22 points against East Tennessee State.
