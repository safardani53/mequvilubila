Frankie &amp; Benny's launch new menu for indoor dining on 17 May - and here's how you can try it

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, March 25, 2024

Frankie & Benny's, one of the UK's most popular chains has announced a new menu.

Hitting restaurants from 17 May, it'll celebrate the return to indoor dining - but don't worry you can still order it home.

The brand new menu includes new, fresh Italian-American dishes and much loved classics that are sure to be a hit with diners.

  • Try Frankie & Benny's new menu from 17 May - click here

The brand new menu will be available at 103 of it's restaurants across the UK, as well as on Uber Eats to enjoy at home if you'd rather.

You can order Frankie & Benny's on Uber Eats here.

With all new items priced under £12.40, it's also perfect for treating the whole family without breaking the bank.

Whether you want to enjoy the comfort food classics at home or dining in, the new menu includes new dishes such as the delicious Buffalo Mac N Cheese Bites, and Vegan twists on old favourites.

We love the sounds of the Vegan BBQ Wingz, which have been made in collaboration with BIFF's - think juicy jackfruit, topped with spring onion and BBQ sauce, and even a sugar cane bone.

There's also the new Meatball Sub, which has been made with delicious pork and beef meatballs, spicy Napoletana tomato sauce, melted cheese, fresh basil and rocket.

We also love the look of the San Francisco Sourdough Pizza's; which will all be hand-stretched to order.

Ben Keggan, Executive Chef at Frankie & Benny’s, said: “We could not be more excited to re-open indoors and welcome customers back with our fresh new menu.

"The team have been working behind the scenes to curate the perfect selection of recipes, made with carefully selected produce and expertly sourced ingredients.

"Guests can expect delicious updates to their favourite comfort foods, as well as new and flavourful dishes such as our brand new BIFF partnership Vegan BBQ Wings, our Mac n Cheese Burger and our Meatball Sub - you’ll struggle to decide!”

You can see the full menu at frankieandbennys.com, and it'll be available to order from Uber Eats from 17 May.
