Kanye West claims ex Kim Kardashian is 'STOPPING' rapper from bringing their kids to Chicago for cha

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, June 6, 2024

KANYE West claimed that his ex Kim Kardashian has allegedly attempted to 'stop' the rapper from bringing their kids to Chicago for a supposed basketball game.

Kanye, 44, shared a rare snapshot of his late mother, Donda West, on his Instagram. 

In the snap from the Netflix documentary, Jeen-yuhs, Ye appeared to be downtrodden while his mom tried to cheer him up. 

His late mother, Donda - who inspired the name for the musician's last album - said: "You need an angel to watch over you."

The Flashing Lights singer captioned the post: "I want to bring my kids to my hometown of Chicago to see my basketball team play for 7 thousand people."

He then claimed: "Kim is stopping that."

The rap star asked: "How is this joint custody?"

Kim, 41, filed for divorce from her former partner back in February 2021.

The former couple share four kids: North, eight, Saint, six, Chicago, four, and Psalm, two.

While Kanye has been publicly criticizing Kim's parenting choices, the Keeping Up With The Kardashians alum recently issued a lengthy statement about his "constant attacks on me in interviews and on social media."

'CONSTANT ATTACKS'

She said in a lengthy statement: “As the parent who is the main provider and caregiver for our children, I am doing my best to protect our daughter while also allowing her to express her creativity in the medium that she wishes with adult supervision because it brings her so much happiness."

Kim went on: “Divorce is difficult enough on our children and Kanye's obsession with trying to control and manipulate our situation so negatively and publicly is only causing further pain for all.

“From the beginning, I have wanted nothing but a healthy and supportive co-parenting relationship because it is what is best for our children.

"And it saddens me that Kanye continues to make it impossible every step of the way."

The E! star concluded: "I wish to handle all matters regarding our children privately and hopefully he can finally respond to the third attorney he has had in the last year to resolve any issues amicably." 

'KIDNAP MY DAUGHTER'

The Power rapper clapped back at Kim's response and slammed her as "the main provider" while voicing his complaints about North using TikTok.

He wrote: "America saw you try to kidnap my daughter on her birthday by not providing the address. You put security on me inside of the house to play with my son. Then accused me of stealing. 

"I had to take a drug test after Chicago’s party cause you accused me of being on drugs Tracy Romulus stop manipulating Kim to be this way."

NEW RELATIONSHIPS?

The reality star has been dating her new man Pete Davidson, 28, ever since they locked lips in a sketch as Aladdin and Jasmine for Saturday Night Live. 

Kanye seemed to have moved on as well as he was spotted in public with actress Julia Fox, 31.

Julia recently told her fans in her Forbidden Fruits podcast that "nothing really happened" between her and Drake after they had gone out on a date back in 2020.
