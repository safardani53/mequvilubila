Kendall Jenner leaves plastic surgeon's in a FULL face mask as Kim Kardashian, Khloe &amp; Kylie admit t

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, April 21, 2024

KENDALL Jenner was seen leaving a plastic surgeon’s office wearing a full face mask.

The spotting comes as her sisters Kim Kardashian, Khloe and Kylie Jenner have admitted to getting work done.

In photos exclusively obtained by The Sun, The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star kept her head down as she walked out of a building where plastic surgeon Dr Jason B Diamond has an office.

Kendall's face was fully covered with a white collagen mask, while she seemed to further conceal her identity by wearing dark sunglasses.

The 25-year-old's hair was pulled back into a low ponytail and she kept warm in a brown fleece zip-up jacket.

It is not clear what procedure or treatment Kendall got done by the doctor, as the office performs facials, botox, fillers, skin tightening, face lift, nose jobs and more.

While all of Kendall’s famous sisters have admitted to having work done, the model has consistently denied claims of plastic surgery.

KENDALL DENIES PLASTIC SURGERY CLAIMS

Back in 2017, Kendall addressed claims she had plastic surgery during an Instagram Live video.

While talking to her fans, the TV personality said: "All of a sudden, photos of us came out with headlines like, ‘OMG Kendall got her lips done and got full facial reconstruction — look at her cheekbones, look at her nose!’

"I was like, this is CRAZY. I didn’t even address it at the time. Because if I address it, people are going to be like, ‘Oh, so she’s defending herself — she must be guilty.’”

She concluded: “As a model, why would I have my face reconstructed? It doesn’t even make sense.”

Kendall isn't the only family member to visit the doctor, as the Beverly Hills-based surgeon has spoke about working with the famous family in the past and was even visited by Scott Disick, 38, in May.

Unlike Kendall, other members of the Kardashian clan have been open about going under the knife.

KYLIE'S PLASTIC SURGERY

During a 2019 YouTube video, Kylie, 24, addressed fan questions about the work she’s had done.

While she denied having breast implants and a nose job, the makeup mogul has been open about using lip fillers.

In an episode of her reality show Life of Kylie, she revealed to having lip fillers due to "insecurities" with her appearance.

She continued to openly talk about the topic on KUWTK and even discussed the motivation for the first time she got fillers during the series finale reunion in June.

During the episode, she told moderator Andy Cohen: "I think my love for makeup started with my insecurity with my lips.

"I had really small lips, and I didn't ever think about it until I had one of my first kisses and a guy said to me, 'Oh my God, you're such a good kisser, but you have such small lips,' or something like that.

"From then on, I felt unkissable." 

KIM'S PROCEDURES

Kim, 41, has also been known to use procedures to change her appearance.

The SKIMS mogul has admitted to using fillers and botox on her face over the years.

Back in 2017, plastic surgeon Dr David Shafer told Us Weekly that the mother of four has used procedures to create "a softer, more refined look."

The doctor said: "Her cheeks appeared filled — likely a product such as Voluma or Vollure. Around her eyes also looks filled, which gives a smooth and youthful appearance.

"She likely has dermal filler in her lips. She likely has also had thermal energy-based treatments, which tightens and lifts the deeper tissue of the cheeks along the jawline and the neck. We also have to note that her makeup has become more sophisticated and professional compared to her earlier images."

KHLOE GOES UNDER THE KNIFE

More recently, Kendall's sister Khloe, 37, explained how the family's introduction to fame led her to become insecure with her appearance.

During the KUWTK reunion in June, the mother of one said being called "fugly" by fans lowered her confidence.

Khloe said: "That's when I became hard on myself because I was like, 'Oh, this is how other people perceived me.'

"I became insecure because of everyone else telling me."

She ultimately decided to go under the knife, stating: "For me, everyone says, 'Oh my gosh, she's had her third face transplant,' but I've had one nose job."

After confirming she's had plastic surgery, Khloe added: "And everyone gets so upset, like, why don't I talk about it. No one's ever asked me."

She also admitted to trying injections, though said she "responded horribly to Botox."

Back in April, Fabulous Digital spoke to a cosmetic surgeon who believes the KUWTK star has spent $50,000 on her face to pull off her glamorous new look.

KOURTNEY'S BOOB JOB

Kourtney, 42, has also gone under the knife, though she said she regrets it.

The eldest Kardashian has been open about her past boob job, which she got done in college.

“I’ve realized that I was made to look a certain way and I’m considering removing them."
