Donald Trump In Black Barbies Lyrics Fears Hell Deport Her Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 22, 2024

“Island girl, Donald Trump want me to go home,” Nicki Minaj, 33, sings in the second verse of “Black Barbies.” The rapper is obviously referring to the 2016 president-elect’s immigration policy, which worries her since she was born in Trindad & Tobago, rather than the United States, although she’s an American citizen

Trump has made it clear that he is going to be incredibly strict about immigration laws, detaining and deporting anyone who’s here illegally and even building a wall to keep Mexican immigrants out. “Now I’m prayin’ all my foreigns don’t get deported,” she raps later on in the verse. Nicki is clearly NOT on team-Trump, but while many celebs were vocal about their disdain for the GOP candidate during the election, she noticeably kept quiet on social media all week long. In fact, she event went on a hiatus from Nov. 7-14, and on the 15th, she finally returned to surprise fans with “Black Barbies.”
