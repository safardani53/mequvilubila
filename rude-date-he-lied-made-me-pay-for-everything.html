I had such a rude date he lied and made me pay for everything, but that's not the worst part

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, March 20, 2024

SOMETIMES, you just know that a date is a fail from the start.

This was certainly the case for one woman who said she had an awful dating experience during which her date lied and made her pay for everything.

The woman told her story to content creator Maz Mirzadeh, who randomly went up to her and asked her to spill the details of her worst dating experience.

She began her story by saying she and her date visited Thorpe Park, an amusement park in Surrey, England.

According to Maz, going to Thorpe Park was the first mistake on the date.

“He must have told me before the date that he drives,” the woman recalled. 

 “Now it comes to the date, he’s saying, he’s giving 10 million excuses why he can’t drive us there.”

The woman then claimed that once they met up at the metro station, the man said his card was “blocked” and therefore could not pay for the ticket.

This also meant she paid for everything on the entire date.

“He’s choosing like the most expensive foods to get,” she explained.

“I remember we went to KFC … he chose the most expensive thing on the menu, he made it a large.

“And then I got to the payment part and he said: ‘Ah, are you not going to donate to charity?’”

Sadly, that wasn't the worst part of the date.

The woman continued on to say that her date repeatedly asked her ego-driven questions like: “Are you happy you’ve finally met me?”

She said she couldn’t wait to get away from the guy and confirmed that she never saw him again.

That was also the story for one woman whose date was so awful, she had him pull over and drop her off at a grocery store parking lot so she could get away from him.

Similarly, another person told her date she never wanted to see him again after he invited her to his office holiday party only to ignore her the entire time to hang out with another female colleague.
