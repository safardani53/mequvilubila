Woman revamps her kitchen for 31 using trendy worktop stickers and grey B&amp;Q paint

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, February 3, 2024

A WOMAN has managed to revamp her entire kitchen – by changing her cupboards and surfaces – for just £31.

If you’ve been putting off redoing your kitchen because of costs, one woman has shared the incredible transformation she achieved on a budget.

Posting on Facebook group Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK Group, created by thrifty mum Holly Smith, she listed the materials she used for the job.

Using paint from B&Q and counter stickers, she updated her kitchen to look chic with grey cupboards and fresh worktops.

She wrote: “Thank you so much to the person who revamped their kitchen.

“I did the exact same but with different colour scheme all for £31.

“Paint is b and q multi surface in the colour ‘Delaware’ £18 and the worktop was [done] using their sticky back plastic £6.50 a roll.”

Her post has been liked more than 2,000 times, as others were inspired to do some DIY in their own kitchen.

One person said: “This looks brilliant!”
