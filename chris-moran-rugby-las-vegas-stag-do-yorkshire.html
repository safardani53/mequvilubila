TRIBUTES have been paid to a popular rugby player who died in a stag do horror crash in Las Vegas.

Chris Moran, 32, was killed instantly after being struck by a lorry as he crossed a freeway in Sin City with pals at 5am on Friday.

Sales director Chris, of Wakefield, West Yorkshire, suffered catastrophic injuries and died upon impact, according to US police.

The southbound I-15, which runs through the main strip, was shut off between Flamingo Road and Tropicana Avenue while emergency services tended to Chris.

Cops and witnesses tried to administer first aid at the roadside, but he was pronounced dead.

Last night, his devastated girlfriend, Georgia Cooke, said: "I don’t even have the words of how I’m feeling right now.
