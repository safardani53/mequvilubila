Who is Sadio Mane?

Sadio Mane is a Senegalese professional footballer who plays as a forward or winger for the Saudi Pro League club Al Nassr and the Senegal national team. Sadio is also known for his speed, pressing, and dribbling. He is one of the greatest players in the world and among the greatest African players of all time.

He was inspired to play football when he saw his country’s national team win the World Cup in 2002. Mane has played internationally for Senegal leading the team to the 2012 Olympic quarterfinals.

Moreover, he represented his nation in the 2012 Fifa World Cup and the 2015 and 2017 African Cup of Nations. According to New African magazines, he was one of the top 100 most influential Africans in 2020.

Additionally, he was involved in many charity works he donated to build a school and hospital in his hometown. Apart from his football career, he is active on his online social media account, which has a huge fan following.

Early Life of Sadio Mane

Sadio Mane was born on 10 April 1992 in Bambali, Senegal in a poor family. When he was seven years old, he lost his father, who was the imam. After his father’s death, his mother gave him the love of both his parents. Sadio has full faith in the Muslim religion.

Since an early age, he had to desire to become a big footballer and make your country proud. At the age of twelve, Mane ran away from his village with the help of his childhood friend Luc Djiboune and moved to Dakar to pursue his dream of becoming a football player. At that time his family members had full support for him.

In his childhood, he was unable to attend school because his parents didn’t have enough money to educate their son. However, he has not revealed anything about his further education by himself.

Sadio Mane Career

Sadio Mane began his professional career debut by taking the position of a French team Metz. In his first season in the league, he made nineteen matches. He relocated to the Austrian football team Red Bull Salzburg in August 2012.

On 31 October, he scored the team’s first hat trick, giving them the win in the Austrian Cup third round. In the Austrian Bundesliga, he kept up his outstanding play inspiring his club to win.

During the 2014-2015 premium league season, Mane moved to Southampton for a record 11.8 million pounds. In his debut matches, he helped his club debut Arsenal 2-1.

He continued to lead the team in scoring and contributed to their many victories this season. By the end of his seasons, he was the team’s top scorer. In a 6-1 victory over Aston Villa in May 2015, Sadio scored three goals smashing the previous premier league record for the fastest hat-trick.

Later, he joined the Liverpool football club for a transfer fee of $34 million in June 2016 where he performed very well. In August 2017, he scored Liverpool’s first goal season in a 3-3 tie with Watford.

He also performed very well in the games that followed winning the August 2017 Premier League Player of the Month Award. In 2021, he scored his 100th goal for Liverpool and his 100th goal in the premier league. With at least 100 goals, he is the third African goal-scorer in Premier League history.

Sadio started his international career in 2012 for Senegal in the Olympics. Due to his standing performance, he guided the team to the quarterfinals where Mexico finished victorious.

Mane’s breakout performance came in the 2021 Africa Cup of Nations where he helped Senegal win. During that moment he helped his team win the championship for the first time, he was named the tournament’s best player.

Throughout his career, he created his Instagram account where he has more than 16.3 million followers. Additionally, he is active on his Facebook account where he has 17 million followers, and uploads many pictures and videos for his fans.
