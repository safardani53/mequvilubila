Ariel Winter Wearing Tight Clothes Pics Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

As Alex Dunphy, Ariel Winter has delighted television audiences for more than ten years as part of the hit comedy, ‘Modern Family.’ Yet, as she’s grown up, so have her tastes in clothes. As a young woman, Ariel has developed a fondness for skintight clothing that hugs her curves in all the right ways. Be it on the red carpet or for running errands, Ariel’s outfits have hugged every one of her curves while showing off all the hard work she’s put in at the gym. Though she has faced body-shaming from some online trolls, she is not changing her wardrobe for anyone. ‘Why can’t people just let other people feel good about themselves and do what they want?’ she said in 2017. ‘WEAR WHATEVER YOU WANT PEOPLE! As long as you feel good about yourself that’s what matters. I know I did. Don’t ever let anyone stifle who you are and how [to] express yourself.’
