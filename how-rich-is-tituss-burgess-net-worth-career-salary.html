How Rich Is Tituss Burgess? Net Worth, Career, Salary

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, April 13, 2024

Best in Snow, Disney Plus’ latest competition program, will premiere on the platform on Friday, November 18, 2022. With the Christmas holidays approaching, the program will enhance viewers’ holiday spirit by mesmerizing them with various sights and imagery. The teams will compete in a Snowy Village and take part in a contest to see who can make the finest snow sculpture and win the championship.

Tituss Burgess, an actor and musician, will present Best in Snow. He has been cast as “Mayor Frostifer” of the Land of the Snowdome. He was born in Athens, Georgia in February 1979 and has been in a number of Broadway and television plays. On the program, the actor has a lovely charisma that will keep fans glued to their television screens.

The new Disney+ series will feature five teams tasked with transforming twenty-ton slabs of snow into gorgeous masterpieces. Pixar, Marvel, Walt Disney Animation, Walt Disney Studios, and The Muppets Studios will all be inspirations for these. Several Disney characters, including Moana, Coco, and The Lion King, will be brought to life by the cast members.

Tituss Burgess’s net worth

Tituss Burgess, the presenter of Best in Snow, has a net worth of $5 million as of December 2023. The actress has been in a number of Broadway shows, including Good Vibrations, Jersey Boys, The Little Mermaid, and Guys and Dolls. He is best known as Titus Andromedon in the television series Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt (2015). Tituss was nominated for an Emmy in 2018 for the same role. He appeared in the series with Ellie Kemper, Jane Krakowski, and Carol Kane. It follows a young lady adapting to life in New York City after 15 years in a cult.

His notable work in the series garnered him a SAG Award for Outstanding Performance by a Male Actor in a Comedy Series, in addition to an Emmy nomination. Burgess also received two Critics Choice Television Awards for Best Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series. He has also received numerous television prizes, including the Webby and Gold Derby TV awards in 2015. From 2011 through 2012, the Best in Snow presenter was initially presented to the television demographic as D’Fwan in the NBC comedy 30 Rock. Other television productions he has worked on include The Battery’s Down, A Gifted Man, Blue Bloods, and Royal Pains. Tituss has also provided his voice for two big Sony studio films, The Angry Birds Movie and Smurfs: The Lost Village. Tituss has a “celebrated high tenor voice and is an accomplished singer and songwriter, headlining major orchestras and top cabaret venues across the globe,” according to The Kennedy Center.

He has also appeared in other charity shows. Among them is the Broadway for Obama benefit concert in Easton, Pennsylvania, the Broadway After Dark benefit event in New York City, and the Broadway Cares/ Equity Fights AIDS fundraiser concert. In 2012, the Best in Snow presenter launched his solo music career with his first album Comfortable. He authored the words and created the music for the majority of the album’s tracks. Tituss’ other works include the 2019 song 45 (with Daniel J Watts) and the 2020 single Dance M.F (featuring Imani Coppola). In 2019, he also released his EP Saint Tituss.
