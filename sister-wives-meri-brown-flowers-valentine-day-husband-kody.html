Sister Wives' Meri Brown sends herself flowers for Valentine's Day after star admits she's 'estrange

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 31, 2024

SISTER Wives' Meri Brown sent herself flowers for Valentine's Day after the reality star admitted that she is "estranged" from her husband, Kody Brown.

Meri, 50, added a laugh track to her sweet Instagram video. 

In the short clip, the Sister Wives star held a large bouquet of flowers in her hands.

Meri lip-synched herself to the audio: "Oh my gosh! Someone sent me flowers!

"I wonder who it could be?"

While reading off the white note, she mimicked the line: "Me!" with a huge smile on her face 

Meri captioned the post: "Sometimes you just gotta bring your own sunshine!"

She concluded: "Happy Valentine's Day!" with pink heart emojis.

Meri's mood was entirely different when she spoke about her estrangement with her former partner Kody, 53.

'HAPPY WITH MYSELF'

The TLC star recently opened up about her feelings towards her husband in a lengthy Instagram post.

Meri wrote: "A few months ago, I used the word 'estranged' regarding my relationship, in a conversation with a friend. It was the first time I had vocalized it, and it felt... honest.

"It was hard to use, and yet somewhat freeing. I know where my relationship is, I'm not oblivious to that. I will always look for the best in people and situations and hope they see it too."

She continued: "The truth is, I know my value. I know my worth. Who I am, what I want in life, what I aspire to be, and what I feel like I'm doing a pretty dang good job at, is being happy."

While being "labeled" by viewers, the TV personality was described as "desperate, pathetic, embarrassing."

She added: "What you've seen is me using my voice to have fun and bring humor to a not-so-perfect situation."

In conclusion, Meri was "happy with myself."

She shared a photo of herself sitting on the windowsill and smiling at the camera.

LOSING CHRISTINE

In addition to being divorced from Meri, Kody is also married to Janelle, 52, and Robyn, 43.

After months of split rumors, Christine, 48, confirmed in November 2021 that she and Kody, 52, had ended their marriage.

She released a statement on social media and said: "After more than 25 years together, Kody and I have grown apart and I have made the difficult decision to leave."

'WE ALL NEED SUPPORT'

Recently, Christine appeared to have celebrated Valentine's Day as well as she shared her "gratitude" with fans. 

With a heart-shaped cushion hanging from the wall behind her, Kody's ex blew a kiss at the camera.
