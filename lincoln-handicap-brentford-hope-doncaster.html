Richard Hughes warns Lincoln fancy Brentford Hope will need outing as Flat season curtain raiser nea

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, September 7, 2024

RICHARD HUGHES is ready to let stable star Brentford Hope do the talking on the track this summer.

His four-year-old is as short as 6-1 for Saturday’s Unibet Lincoln at Doncaster, the annual curtain-raiser for the Flat season.

But the three-time Champion Jockey turned trainer has warned punters Brentford Hope will come on for his first outing of the campaign.

Hughes said: “There’s always been a lot of talk around this horse, but I’ll try and let the horse do the talking from now on.

“He’s going to run on Saturday, but he’ll come on for it.”

Only Charlie Appleby’s Eastern World and John and Thady Gosden’s Haqeeqy are shorter in the betting, at 3-1 and 7-2.
