Justin Bieber Hold Tight Description For Selena Gomez? Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, February 25, 2024

Justin Bieber References Selena Gomez In Song Description

Justin’s new song hit iTunes on Oct. 20, and it was yet another love song! However, he wasn’t quite sure how he wanted to describe it — he changed the caption three times.

His first caption read:

This song is about the one you can’t shake. I am a hopeless romantic so when I love someone, I never want to let them go. This song is about the rush you get when you have that feeling. No matter how hard you try, you can’t let the person go. You just want to hold on as tight as you can. I tried to capture that with this one. –JB

Afterward, he realized he had more to add.

And one more thing: it is never a bad thing to make a lady feel something inside she has never felt before. Make her feel like a real lady. Hold tight girl ;)

However, he later erased it all and simply wrote:

You really want me to describe this song? I told you the journals are bringing you into my most intimate moments. Just listen to the song and HOLD on as TIGHT as you can. Happy Birthday.

Justin’s Loving Lyrics To Selena

While Justin isn’t currently with Selena, he’s definitely trying through his song lyrics.

“Don’t let this go to your head, but you’re the best I’ve ever had,” he sings, before getting a little more sensual with his lyrics.

Before “Hold Tight,” he released was “All That Matters,” which might as well have been a love letter for her.

“Whenever you’re not in my presence, it feels like I’m missing my blessings, yeah. So I sleep through the daylight, stay awake all night ’til you’re back again,” he sung in the song.

So HollywoodLifers, do you think Justin is trying to get Selena’s attention with his songs? Let us know!
