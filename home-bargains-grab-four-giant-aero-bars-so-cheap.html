Chocolate lovers scramble to Home Bargains to grab four GIANT Aero bars that are so cheap people are

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, February 1, 2024

SHOPPERS are rushing to grab a deal on Aero chocolate bars so good they're picking up five boxes at a time.

For those last-minute shoppers you'll want to listen up as the bargain deal contains four giant sized Aero chocolate bars for just £1.

A savvy shopper shared the bargain she spotted in store and people were very excited to nab it in time for Christmas.

Posting to the popular Facebook group, Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, which boasts over 2million members they shared a picture of the box.

The Aero selection box comes with four flavours - milk chocolate, mint chocolate, orange chocolate, and vanilla.

"These size bars normally sell over £1 each," the bargain-hunter wrote.
