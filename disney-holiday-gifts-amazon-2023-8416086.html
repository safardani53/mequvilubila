Joy Jolt Disney Espresso Glass Cup, $21

For the coffee lover, check out this espresso cup made from double-wall glass that has an optical 3D design of Mickey Mouse, complete with an iridescent sheen for an added magical touch.

The insulated coffee mug can hold up to 5.4 ounces and has a wide handle for easy carrying. Plus, it’s on sale for $21 right now.

Corkcicle Disney Princess Belle Tumbler, $34 with Prime

Another solid drinkware option someone who wants to channel their inner Disney princess can take on the go is this Corkcicle tumbler. Its light blue, white, and gold design pays homage to Beauty and the Beast, as it has images of Belle and the Beast, the Beast’s castle, Belle’s cottage, Lumiere, Cogsworth, and roses placed throughout.

The reusable cup can keep beverages hot or cold for up to five hours. One shopper called it “smooth, easy to hold, functional, and cute.”

For those to like to wear their Disney pride, check out this dark orange crewneck sweater that has an image of a smiling Mickey Mouse, and a classic black T-shirt with a small Mickey Mouse in the left corner.

Disney 100 Collectible Mickey and Minnie Mouse Figures, $25

When shopping for the little ones, you can never go wrong with anything Disney, including these Mickey and Minnie Mouse figures. They toys come with swappable heads and hands, so kids ages six and up can bring Mickey and Minnie to life and have the toys hold hands and share a sweet smooch. 

“My son and I have played with all the parts so far. Great for any Disney lover, and they are great quality,” one shopper wrote.

Disney 100th Anniversary Metallic Platinum Rubik’s Cube, $15

Looking for a solid stocking stuffer that will keep someone entertained for hours? The classic Rubik’s Cube gets a Disney makeover as each side has beloved Disney characters, including Woody, Elsa, Snow White, and Mickey Mouse.

The 3D puzzle is suitable for long car rides and office appointments where wait times can be long, and its metallic hue gives it more of a decorative touch, so it can be displayed on a shelf once completed.

Lego Disney Classic 100 Years of Disney Animation Icons, $48

And for an activity that the family can get in on together, this Lego set allows builders to create up to 72 different Disney designs (don’t worry, it comes with an instruction booklet!) on 12 plates.

The plates can be placed inside the framed collage, which you can hang on a wall and display. The set comes with a minifigure of Mickey Mouse, who is holding a paintbrush and palette, too. 

Disney iPhone 14 Mickey and Minnie Mouse Case, $22

Everyone needs a reliable phone case, but it’s not something that you often think to change until you need a new one. Gift the Disney fan on your list with this baby blue phone case that is monogrammed “M,” in honor of Mickey and Minnie, as Minnie plants a kiss on Mickey’s cheek. 

Not only is it cute, but it’s made from a scratch-resistant material and has a shell shock absorbent liner to prevent damage in case of a drop.
