Kelly Clarkson Latest News and Updates

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, February 10, 2024

Singer and talk show host Kelly Clarkson shot to fame after winning the very first season of American Idol in 2002. Shortly after, she released her debut album "Thankful," which was followed by her second album "Breakaway."

She went on to release three more albums over the years, which featured her hit singles "My Life Would Suck Without You" and "Stronger (What Doesn't Kill You Makes You Stronger)." In total, Kelly has released eight albums and sold over 25 million albums worldwide.

Kelly joined The Voice cast as a judge in 2017 and also began hosting her own daytime talk show in 2019. Kelly married talent manager Brandon Blackstock in 2013, and the couple welcomed two kids — River and Remington — together, before splitting in 2020. 

Full Name: Kelly Brianne Clarkson

Relationship Status: Single (previously married to Brandon Blackstock)
