I'm an ex-Chick-fil-A employee - any soda can be 'frosted' for a few bucks and its definitely

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, February 11, 2024

A CHICK-Fil-A employee has revealed that any of the restaurant's drinks can be frosted if you follow some simple rules.

Chick-Fil-A is known for some of its frosted drinks, including its Frosted Lemonade and frozen coffee treats.

An employee has revealed that the restaurant can make these drinks in any flavor if you follow some rules.

The video was shared on TikTok by Morgan Chomps (@morganchompz).

"Did you know you can get any drink frosted at Chick-Fil-A?" begins the video, which shows a woman entering the restaurant where her sister works.

"All you have to do is ask if you can have any drink that you choose frosted, whether it be their sodas or their teas," continues the video.

"I'm going to get a frosted Fanta to show you."

She continues to explain that not all Chick-Fil-A locations will do this, since these items are not officially on the menu.

"Just ask politely, and if they can't, please respect that," she concludes the video.

Viewers on TikTok shared their own stories and even tried out her trick with success.

A user called Brother D.D. Dukes (@brotherdukes) shared his own video featuring his frosted drink of choice.

"I just got the HI-C," he says to the camera.

He tries the drink and shares his approval.

"Y'all gonna have to try this!" he said.

"This is frosted HI-C."

FROSTED DRINKS ARE TIKTOK FAMOUS

It appears like frosted drinks have become famous thanks to TikTok, with more people ordering them in stores.

Dukes shared that the moment he approached the counter and asked for the drink, the Chick-Fil-A employee knew he was trying out a TikTok trend.

"As soon as I went up to the register, and I asked them, she was like, 'You saw this on TikTok, didn't you?'"
