How many kids does Ginger Zee have?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, May 27, 2024

GOOD Morning America co-host Ginger Zee is known for providing the show's viewers with weather updates.

The meteorologist is also a mother to her children with her husband Ben Aaron.

How many kids does Ginger Zee have?

From her marriage to Ben Aaron, Ginger Zee has two children; sons Adrian and Miles.

On June 29, 2015, Ginger announced she and Ben are expecting their first child together.

At the time, she told GMA viewers: "It's so emotional for me. The forecast for delivery is in December."

The news anchor said she was wondering about the right time to make her pregnancy announcement because she thought fans could tell she was beginning to show signs.
