Marvel Stars Dating Histories: Chris Evans, More

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, April 13, 2024

Superheroes want love too! The stars of the Marvel Cinematic Universe have not only connected meaningfully on screen, but they also found romance in their real lives.

Black Widow actress Scarlett Johansson married Colin Jost in October 2020, more than a decade after she joined the MCU. Although the Saturday Night Live loves Marvel, but Johansson revealed in June 2021 that he didn’t want any inside scoop.

“He read comics growing up,” Johansson explained on SiriusXM’s “The Jess Cagle Podcast With Julia Cunningham” at the time. “He doesn’t like any spoilers though, so I can’t tell him anything.” 

Meanwhile, other Marvel stars have found on-set romances. Tom Holland and Zendaya play love interests Peter and MJ, respectively, in the Spider-Man films. After years of relationship rumors, Holland and Zendaya were spotted kissing in his car in July 2021.
