Is Kenny Omega Gay? Sexuality &amp; Relationship Revealed

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

While the AEW star Kenny Omega is hailed as one of the best professional wrestlers in the world, his sexuality has remained shrouded in mystery. Is Kenny Omega Gay? Fans ask. 

Kenny Omega recently made headlines as WWE Hall Of Famer Kevin Nash compared Omega to his former WCW stablemate Sean Waltman.

In this article, we’ll explore Kenny’s relationship status while answering the questions concerning his sexuality.

Tyson Smith, better known by his ring name Kenny Omega, has made waves in the world of professional wrestling.

Omega is a Canadian-born wrestler with an impressive list of accolades. His journey through the world of sports entertainment has been nothing short of remarkable.

The Canadian made his professional debut in the Winnipeg-based promotion Premier Championship Wrestling in 2001.

From his time in New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW) to his current role at AEW, Omega has solidified his place as one of the industry’s top talents.

Omega held titles like the IWGP Heavyweight Championship and IWGP United States Heavyweight Championship in NJPW.

Moreover, he has also held the AEW World Championship, AEW World Tag Team Championship, and AEW World Trios Championship.

Is Kenny Omega Gay?

Amidst the accolades and achievements, one aspect of the wrestling icon’s life remains well under the radar – Kenny Omega’s sexuality.

Although Omega is a public figure, he has been remarkably private about his personal life and relationships.

Speculation about his sexual orientation has swirled for years, especially during his partnership with Kota Ibushi.

Omega formed the “Golden Lovers” tag team with Ibushi during his early NJPW days.

The Golden Lovers’ on-screen dynamic led to questions about their real-life relationship, sparking rumors about Omega being gay.

However, when asked about the nature of their partnership in an interview with Yahoo Sports, Omega chose to leave it open-ended, saying, “Let people think what they want to think.”

He emphasized that he has no problem with the LGBTQ+ community viewing the Golden Lovers as their team.

However, despite the speculation and occasional innuendo, Kenny Omega’s actual sexuality remains undisclosed.

It’s important to remember that everyone has the right to keep their personal life private, and Kenny Omega is no exception.

He has never publicly confirmed whether he identifies as gay, bisexual, straight, or any other sexual orientation.

Kenny Omega Relationship

The “Best Bout Machine” has kept his mouth shut not only about his sexuality but also about his relationships.

Although Omega hasn’t officially spoken anything about his relationship, he is often linked with his co-stars from inside the ring.

Not only is his name associated with his tag-team partner but also with the female stars of the industry.

Aside from Kota Ibushi, there have also been rumors linking Omega to fellow AEW stars like Hikaru Shida and Riho.

Rumors had that he was in a relationship with former AEW Women’s Champion Hikaru Shida.

In addition, some fans theorized that Omega was with Riho due to their relationship at the beginning of AEW. 

Meanwhile, Riho has a long friendship with Omega, going back to their days wrestling together in Japan.

However, these rumors are largely unsubstantiated. There is no concrete evidence to suggest that Omega was in a romantic relationship with either of them.

It’s worth noting that in the world of professional wrestling, fans often engage in speculation and fantasy booking. And sometimes rumors can take on a life of their own.
