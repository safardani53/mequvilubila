Rihanna Shows Nipples &amp; New Pink Hair At NBA Playoff Game

The Clippers lost in game six of the series to the Thunder 104 – 98 but we’re pretty sure some of the fans weren’t even watching the game. Why would fans watch the Clippers earn loss number four in the series when they could see a court side Rihanna show? 

 

RiRi was seen several times jumping out of her seat to cheer, wearing nothing more than a skimpy white tank top, and skinny jeans. She was flashing nipple all night at the crowd and at one time playfully cupped her left boob when the camera panned across the court side seats.

Shortly before she let the world see her pink hair Rihanna tweeted: “We raided Nicki’s [Minaj] wig closet for the summer! Bad gals just wanna have fun!”

We guess she would just “wanna have fun” and maybe take her mind off the rocky relationship she’s had with Drake in the process.
