Burger King announces new flavor twist on 'Royal Crispy' sandwich but there's bad news for fans

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 26, 2024

BURGER King has announced another new meal that will be joining the menu this month.

The fast-food joint is on a roll and launching another new meal this month

Chicken has been getting the royal treatment this month on Burger King's menu.

The restaurant will launch the new Honey Mustard BK Royal Crispy chicken sandwich on August 14, reports Chewboom.

Each sandwich comes stacked with a crispy white meat chicken fillet, sliced tomatoes, lettuce, bacon, honey mustard, and Swiss cheese, on a brioche bun.

The U.S. Sun reached out to Burger King for more information about the new sandwich.

The chicken wrap could potentially satisfy cravings for the long-gone McDonald's Snack Wrap.

Fans haven't been able to order it since 2016, but it appears time hasn't healed this wound.

"2am, laying awake because of my troubles. (Troubles being I need @McDonalds to bring back the snack wrap.)" wrote on fan on X (the social media site formerly known as Twitter).

THE ROYAL TREATMENT

Burger has been testing out a host of new menu items.

The classic Chicken Fries got a major makeover when the restaurant launched the chicken parm and chicken & waffle fries flavors.

The chicken parm fries feature parmesan, garlic, and Italian herbs, and come with a side of marinara dipping sauce.

The chicken & waffle option is coated with waffle batter and served with a side of syrup.

In the past, Burger King has offered tons of takes on Chicken Fries, including buffalo, jalapeno, and pretzel.

Unfortunately, the flavors have all been served for a limited time only.

Fans have voiced their frustration with the constantly changing options, with one saying: "I will never understand @BurgerKing. They release spicy chicken fries. People love them and then a week later. Discontinued. Why?"
