These &quot;Little Miss&quot; Memes Are Taking Over Social Media

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 18, 2024

The latest meme trend to take social media by storm is the "Little Miss" and "Mr. Men" format. Here are a few of our favorite memes!

Check out some of our favorites below!
