'Your mother is a mattress' Aberdeen fans target Rangers star Morelos with offensive banner but get

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 2, 2024

ABERDEEN fans have aimed a tasteless banner at Alfredo Morelos – branding the Rangers striker’s mother a “mattress”.

Morelos was back at Pittodrie for the Scottish Cup quarter-final, having been sent off in all three previous games against the Dons.

But a section of home fans targeted the Colombian striker by unveiling a display, written in Spanish, reading: “Your mother is a mattress”.

A picture of the banner quickly spread across social media and a number of fans were quick to hit out at the offensive display.

One person wrote: "How sad & pathetic to bring a players family into things".

Another commented: "That is f*****g awful patter".

One fan said: "What sort of insult is that".

A fourth raged: "The banner aimed at Morelos was an embarrassing fail but since when has it been okay to target a players mother?"

While one added: "That Aberdeen banner is embarrassing."

SunSport Online has contacted both Aberdeen and Rangers for comment.
