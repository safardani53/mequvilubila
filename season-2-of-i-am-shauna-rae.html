Will there be a season 2 of I Am Shauna Rae?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, April 5, 2024

TLC viewers are waiting to find out if they can enjoy following Shauna Rae for another season.

The season one finale premieres on February 15, 2022.

Who is Shauna Rae?

Shauna Rae is the star of the show I Am Shauna Rae, which debuted on TLC in January 2022.

The series chronicles the struggle that comes with appearing much younger than you are.

Rae, 22, experienced development loss during treatment of a rare brain cancer.

Though she is in her twenties, she stands at 3 feet and 10 inches tall.

According to the show, it's challenging for Rae to make certain lifestyle decisions, such as wearing makeup and adult clothing in public.

Not all of Rae's challenges are superficial. She also experiences challenges related to dating and fertility.

Where did Shauna Rae grow up?

Shauna Rae was born and raised in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania where she currently lives with her family. This is also the backdrop of the show.

Shauna has spent her entire life in Pittsburgh.

In an effort to become more independent, she discusses moving up to New York in a few episodes.

Her family, who is featured in the show, includes two half-sisters, Rylee and Morgan.

She has a sister, Tara, who lives in Florida.

Will there be a second season?

In the lead-up to the February 15, 2022 season finale, the network has not confirmed a second season.

The network has also been silent on any cancelation.

News of a second season may depend on what transpires in the final episode.

What lies ahead for Shauna Rae?

In an interview with Hollywood Life, Shauna Rae shared that her life today isn't a lot different than before the show.

She is still making an effort to move out of her parent's home.

Her dating life is quiet since filming ended, she says.
