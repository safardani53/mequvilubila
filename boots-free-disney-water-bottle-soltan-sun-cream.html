Bargain hunter shares how to get a free Disney water bottle from Boots - but you'll need to be quick

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, April 21, 2024

A BARGAIN hunter has shared how you can nab a free Disney water bottle - so if you have a little one and want to get them a treat without having to spend any cash, you’ll need to listen up. 

Savvy shopper Alexandria Martin spotted the freebie Disney bottle when shopping in her local Boots. 

After wanting to buy some sun cream, Alexandria noticed that when you spent £15 or more on Soltan sun cream, you will get a Disney water bottle for free. 

Alexandria proudly took to Facebook to show off her Disney bottle, leaving many stunned. 

Posting in the Facebook group, Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK group, which has 2.2million members, the thrilled shopper shared her sun cream and water bottle. 

Alexandria said: “Not sure if this has already been posted, but if you buy two Soltan products you get a free Disney bottle.”

Alexandria got a Disney bottle with the faces of three Disney characters on it - Mickey Mouse, Minnie Mouse and Pluto.

There’s also a Moana bottle and an Encanto one up for grabs too. 

At the time of writing, Alexandria shared her post just 21 hours prior and it had quickly amassed 212 likes and 106 comments. 

Social media users were eager to nab themselves the free bottle, which many took to the comments to express.
