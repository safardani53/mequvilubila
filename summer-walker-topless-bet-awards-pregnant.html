Summer Walker goes totally TOPLESS at BET Awards red carpet just hours after announcing she's pregna

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, April 2, 2024

SUMMER Walker has turned heads at the BET Awards, walking the red carpet topless, only covering her nipples in gold pasties.

The fashion choice comes a day after the singer announced she's pregnant with her second child.

Summer, 26, who was nominated for Best Female R&B/Pop Artist, walked like an Egyptian down the carpet, in a gold mini skirt, golden symbol pasties and gold earrings.

Just a day earlier, she announced on Instagram Live that she is expecting.

"People asking me if I'm pregnant… I am and you know I'm very, very, very, very, happy about it," she told fans.

"Very excited about it. And this is gonna be - I'm very very excited because it's going to be different from how it was before. It's really peaceful, really happy, lots of help, lots of love."

Summer currently shares a 1-year-old daughter with ex-boyfriend and producer, London on da Track, after being together for two years.

She is now expecting a child with her current lover, LVRD Pharoh, according to BET.

The No Love singer admitted she shared the news to get ahead of the speculation and rumors - something she was unable to do before baby number one.

"The only reason I’m even saying anything is because last time I felt very disrespected that people didn’t let me tell that myself," she continued.

"People were taking pictures of me in the store — I know it kind of comes with the job –… [but] people took pictures of me and sent it to The Shade Room before I could even announce my pregnancy. I thought that was very disrespectful and it made me very, very, very, very, very upset.”

Soon after Summer and her last boyfriend London on da Track broke up, it was rumored that she had a new man in her life.

His identity was later revealed as LVRD Pharaoh Larry, who is known as an American rapper.

In August 2021, Summer first revealed her new new man and while she didn't identify him at the time, the hitmaker said: "My career is doing good, My baby is healthy and beautiful. My man is doing everything that I need him to do plus more. Don't even gotta ask."

Just a few months later, in November 2021, the couple debuted face tattoos which each others names.

"Eternal Love Wins," he captioned the post.

As mentioned, Summer's most high profile relationship was with London, known professionally as London on da Track.

He is an American record producer, rapper and songwriter.

The producer has collaborated with some of rap's biggest names including, Birdman, Lil Wayne, Lil Baby, Drake, Post Malone, 50 Cent and 21 Savage among others.
