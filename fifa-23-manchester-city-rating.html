Manchester City FIFA 23 player ratings in full

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 22, 2024

FIFA 23 doesn’t come out until September 27, for Ultimate fans, but some of the latest Premier League ratings have leaked early.

If you are wondering just what changes have been made to Manchester City in the upcoming game, we have all the info for you here.

What does this mean for Man City players?

On the face of things, most ratings seem unchanged for Man City players.

There is no loss of points while Bernando Silva and Phil Foden pick up a point each.

However, the loss of Gabriel Jesus to Arsenal will be a heavy blow as he was and is a 83-rated player.

It’s certainly not all doom and gloom for City fans, as the squad managed to pick up some fearsome players in return.

Manuel Akanji is expected to do well, but was signed up too late to have figures for.

While lower down the list Kalvin Philips is another great player, keeping Man City’s ratings firmly in the 80s.

The biggest win for the Cityzens is the purchase of Erling Braut Haaland who has already proved his worth to the team by scoring nine goals in the first five games.
