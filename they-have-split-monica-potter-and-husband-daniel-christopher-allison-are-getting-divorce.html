They Have Split: Monica Potter and Husband Daniel Christopher Allison Are Getting Divorce

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, April 25, 2024

They are no more together! Monica Potter and husband Daniel Christopher Allison are getting divorced, multiple sources have confirmed.

According to E! News, court documents filed by Monica cites “irreconcilable differences” as the reason behind their separation. The date of separation has not been noted in the papers.

The alum and orthopedic surgeon married in 2005, and they share a daughter, Molly, 12, together. The actress also has two adult sons, 24-year-old Liam and 28-year-old Daniel, with her former husband Tom Potter, whom she was married to from 1990 to 1998.
