Julia Roberts kisses husband Daniel Moder as they celebrate 18 years of marriage The US Sun

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, March 23, 2024

JULIA Roberts is head over heels in love with her husband Daniel Moder and smothers him with kisses on their 18th wedding anniversary.

The Academy Award winning actress, 52, posted the cute pic to her Instagram and captioned it: "✨18 years✨ #heckyes."

In the adorable post, Daniel is smiling at the camera as Julia plants a kiss at his cheek.

Her blonde hair is pulled back and the star is wearing aviator glasses, a black and white horizontal cotton top and statement necklace.

In only a couple of hours the post racked up hundreds of thousands of likes from Julia's 8.9million followers.

Her fans showered the happy couple with love.

One Instagram user wrote: "Happy Anniversary! You two are truly awesome."
Julia married the cinematographer in 2002 and the couple have three children together.

Twins Hazel and Phinnaeus were born in 2004 and their brother Henry was born three years later in 2007.

The happy pic is rare one of her family as the Erin Brockovich star rarely shares photo of her personal life on Instagram.

Julia's wedding anniversary comes just days after the news broke her niece and fellow actress, Emma Roberts was expecting her first child.

The 29-year-old American Horror Story star is expecting a baby with her boyfriend, Tron actor Garrett Hedlund.

Last week, Us Weekly revealed on their Hot Hollywood podcast that the stars are expecting their first baby together.

Speculation of a romance first began after they were spotted on a walk in the Los Angeles neighborhood of Silver Lake together in March 2019.

Prior to dating, Emma and Garrett, 35, had been friends for years.

The Nickelodeon alum was previously engaged to AHS co-star Evan Peters.

She began dating the actor in 2012 after they met on the set of Adult World.

They went on to work together on multiple seasons of the award-winning Ryan Murphy horror series.


Who are Julia Roberts' celebrity exes?

Julia Roberts is a Hollywood darling who has been in the spotlight since bursting onto the scene in 1988 with the film mystic pizza.

She has since gone on to become one of the commercially and critically successful actors of her generation with multiple award wins inlcuding Best Actress for her role in Erin Brockovich in 2000.

Before marrying Daniel Boder in 2002, Julia dated a string of high profile stars.

She has been linked to Jason Patric, Liam Neeson, Dylan McDermott, Benajmin Bratt, Matthew Perry and Kiefer Sutherland, to whom she was engaged but ended it three days before their wedding.

On June 25, 1993, she married country singer Lyle Lovett, but they later divorced in 1995.
