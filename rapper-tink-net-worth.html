What is Tink's net worth?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, March 27, 2024

TINK is a rising star in the music industry.

The rapper just recently released her newest album, Heat of the Moment, featuring Kodak Black, Yung Bleu, Jeremih, and Davido.

What is Tink's net worth?

Tink's net worth is not immediately well known yet.

Some have reported her net worth to be $4,000 while other have stated it is around $500,000.

Tink released her first album back in 2012 and signed with Timbaland's Mosley Music Group back in 2014 but did not become a house hold name until recently.

She is best known for her 2014 single, Treat Me Like Somebody, and her collaboration with Jeremih on Don't Tell Nobody.

Who is Tink?

Born Trinity Laure'Ale Home, Tink is an American rapper, singer and songwriter.

Born March 18, 1995, Tink is currently 26-years-old.

Tink is a native of Chicago, Illinois and would spend her time recording music there while her debut album with Timbaland was stalled back in 2015.

What happened between Tink and Timbaland?

Tink spent four years in label limbo with her first deal.

Despite hitting it off from the start with Timerbaland, things went south after the label delayed her debut album, Think Tink, back in 2015 and ultimately stalled her career.

“I really had to get a grip on everything in my life so that I could come back and be able to just focus on the music this year,” she told The Fader back in 2018.

"I had dedicated so much time, and we had put in a lot of work together, me and [Timbaland]. We had an album done, but that whole process was just very shocking to me."
