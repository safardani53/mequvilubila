Bloke shows how to make a KFC Chicken Twister wrap at home and its so easy

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

KFC fans missing out on their finger lickin’ fix fear not, a chef has revealed how you can make a Chicken Twister wrap at home.

Aussie YouTube chef, Rob Nixon of Nicko’s Kitchen has revealed the secret to making the signature snack in your very own kitchen and it is so simple.

To get the signature “zingy” taste he begins by adding pepper and tabasco to his mayonnaise.

Rob also adds a total of eight spices to his flour mix to give the batter a signature KFC taste as well as adding buttermilk to his egg wash to keep the chicken extra moist.

After frying the chicken, he adds the mayo as a base to a corn tortilla that has been microwaved for 30 seconds.

After applying the lettuce and tomato garnishes and the chicken, he folds into a wrap before wrapping the bottom in tin foil, making it easier to eat.

Rob says: “This is probably twice the size of a KFC Twister but there’s nothing wrong with that, we’re going big time with these bad boys.”

After tasting it he adds: “Wow that is as close as you’re going to get, I’m so proud of myself.

“Flavour wise it is spot on and these are really simple to do. They’re bigger obviously than the bought one, you just have to taste it to believe it.

“These are that damn good, give them a try, you’re gonna love them.”


KFC TWISTER RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 4 large (or 8 small) chicken tenderloins
  • 4 flour tortillas
  • 1 cup whole egg mayo
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp tabasco
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 2 cups gourmet lettuce leaves

Flour mix

  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 1 tsp salt & pepper
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp ground chili
  • 1 tsp ground paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried sage leaves
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp onion powder

EGG WASH

  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg

Method

Combine you mayonnaise with the pepper and tobasco.

In a shallow dish combine the eggs with the buttermilk and in a separate dish combine your flour and spices.

Coat each tenderloin in the flour mix and then in the egg wash before coating in the flour again.

Heat your oil in a pan, test that it is ready for frying by throwing a piece of bread into the pan - if it bubbles it is ready

Cook the chicken for 5-6 minutes. For a larger piece allow for slightly longer.

Microwave a flour tortilla for 30 seconds in the microwave and gthen spread a rectangular layer of mayo onto the centre.

Add the lettuce and tomato garnish and then lay a piece of chicken to the centre.

Fold from the bottom upwards and then bring each side into the centre.

Wrap tin foil around the bottom and enjoy!
