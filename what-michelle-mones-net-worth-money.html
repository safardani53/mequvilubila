What is Michelle Mone's net worth and how did she make her money?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 3, 2024

MICHELLE Mone has sparked controversy after it was found that she was a beneficiary of a PPE company used by the government.

Mone sits in the House of Lords after being made a Conservative life peer in 2015. But how did Mone make her money? Here's what we know.

What is Michelle Mone's net worth?

Mone's net worth is estimated to be in the region of £20million.

Mone and her three children received a sum of £29million after profiting from a PPE business that was awarded large contracts by the government during the height of the pandemic.

How did Michelle Mone make her money?

Mone left school at the age of 15 with no qualifications. She wanted to pursue a career in modelling.

She initially worked in a marketing role at Labatt brewing company, and within two years was head of marketing in Scotland, despite admitting to falsifying qualifications in order to secure the role.

After being made redundant, Mone co-founded MJM International in 1996. She launched the company with her then-husband Michael.

Mone lauched Ultimo at Selfridges in 1999. Mone had the idea for the brands first product, the Ultimo bra, after wearing an uncomfortable bra at a dinner party, and believed that she could make a more comfortable alternative.

Mone also had ventures into weight loss pills. Trimsecrets was a joint venture between MJM and Dutch naturopath, Jan De Vries, starting in 2006.

De Vries sold his interest in the company in 2013, leaving Mone as the majority stakeholder in the company.

MJM international was dissolved after Mone left the company due to the breakdown of her marriage in 2013.

Mone 80% of her shares in Ultimo in 2014, claiming that she had "Hung up her bra."

Mone then proceeded to buy 100% of shares in Utan, a fake tanning company.

Is Michelle Mone dating anyone and does she have any children?

Mone has been married to Scottish businessman Douglas Barrowman since 2020. The pair had been engaged since 2018.

Mone has three children from her first marriage to businessman Michael Mone.

Her eldest daughter Rebecca is also a businesswoman, and now runs Utan after taking over from her mother.

Mone's youngest daughter Bethany appeared on the Channel 4 documentary Born famous, where she explored what her life could have been like if her mother wasn't successful.
