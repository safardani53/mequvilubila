Types of online bingo games

Sun Bingo offers a number of bingo games. The main difference between them are the number of balls in play.

  • 90-ball  

This format is the traditional game played in UK bingo halls.

The bingo cards appear as a 3x9 grid comprising 15 random numbers.

The aim is to get a line or full house.

  • 80-ball  

This game format appears as a 4x4 grid comprising 16 random numbers.

The aim is to get a vertical, horizontal or diagonal line. Seen as the perfect middle between 90-ball and 75-ball bingo, 80-ball bingo has surged in popularity in recent years.

Just like 80-ball bingo, the aim is to get a vertical, horizontal or diagonal line.

This game format appears as a 5x5 grid comprising 24 random numbers (the middle square is left blank).

How to win online bingo

There are a few ways to win when playing online bingo, and how much you are awarded will depend on your type of win:

  • 1 line – a row, column or diagonal line of squares has been marked off your ticket.
  • 2 lines – two rows, columns or diagonal lines of squares have been marked off your ticket.
  • Full House – every square has been marked off your ticket
  • Pattern – a set winning pattern of squares has been marked off your ticket

Jackpot

Bingo jackpots are triggered if the full house is won before a specific number of calls.

The number of calls that the full house must be won before for the jackpot to be activated varies between the different bingo rooms.
