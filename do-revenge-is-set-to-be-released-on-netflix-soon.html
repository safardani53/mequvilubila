Do Revenge Is Set To Be Released On Netflix Soon

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, February 21, 2024

Do Revenge, Netflix’s latest dark comedy film will be released on the site on September 16, 2022. The film, directed by Jennifer Kaytin Robinson, is inspired in part by Alfred Hitchcock’s 1951 masterpiece, Strangers on a Train. The plot revolves around two high school girls who plan to get vengeance on each other’s bullies. Camila Mendes and Maya Hawke play the primary parts, with numerous others playing important supporting ones. Without further ado, continue reading to learn about Do Revenge’s release date, storyline, and other facts.

Do Revenge on Netflix release date, trailer, what to anticipate, and more information

Do Revenge is set to premiere on Netflix on Friday, September 16, 2022, at 12 a.m. PT. On August 9, 2022, Netflix published the film’s official trailer, which featured Camila Mendes’ Drea Torres stating,

“I diligently built the ideal existence for 17 years.” I had the ideal friends and the ideal partner. But do you want to know where all of this got me? “Totally demolished.”

“Max wrecked my life,” she says as she punches her lover. He’s never going to get away with this.” The narrative is formed later when Drea tells her friend Eleanor that they should “join up and execute each other’s retribution.”

Overall, the teaser has a whimsical tone that lovers of dark comedy will undoubtedly like. The plot seems fascinating, and it will be interesting to watch how it unfolds. Along with the trailer, Netflix released a brief synopsis of the film, which reads:

“After a secret meeting, Drea (Alpha, the fallen it girl portrayed by Camila Mendes) and Eleanor (beta, the new alt girl played by Maya Hawke) band up to track down each other’s tormentors. Do Revenge is a twisted Hitchcockian dark comedy with the most terrifying protagonists of all: young females.”

Strangers on a Train, an Alfred Hitchcock picture that inspired the film in part, has a similar narrative. In the film, one guy, a psychopath, presents a terrifying “exchange vengeance” scheme to the other. Although the picture received mixed reviews at the time, it is today considered acknowledged as one of Hitchcock’s best works.

A short peek at the cast of Do Revenge

Camila Mendes plays the protagonist character, Drea Torres, who sets out to get vengeance on her lover for humiliating her. Camila looks fantastic in the clip, effortlessly displaying the many shades of her character. The very accomplished actress is expected to provide a fierce performance. Mendes is most recognized for her role as Veronica Lodge on Riverdale, which she played before Do Revenge. She has also appeared in the films Palm Springs, The Perfect Date, and Dangerous Lies.

Maya Hawke also appears in the film as Eleanor, a schoolgirl who is often tormented. Hawke looks great in the clip and has a distinct sense of comedy that raises the picture to a higher level. Hawke has featured in a number of films and television episodes, including Stranger Things, Mainstream, and Quentin Tarantino’s Once Upon a Time in Hollywood. Aside from Hawke and Mendes, the film also features Austin Abrams, Talia Ryder, and Eliza Bennett in key supporting parts, among many more. Do Revenge will be available on Netflix on September 16, 2022.
