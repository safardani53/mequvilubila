Where is the cast of All That now?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, May 19, 2024

CAST your mind back and you may well remember the Nickelodeon sketch comedy series All That.

But fans of the show - which aired between 1994 and 2005 - may wonder where the cast of the hit series are now.

Where are the cast of All That now?

Amanda Bynes

Amanda Bynes worked as a child actress on All That from 1996 to 2000, as well as starring in its spin-off series The Amanda Show.

She later appeared in successful films including What A Girl Wants, Robots, and She's The Man.

In 2010 she starred in the comedy-drama Easy A alongside Emma Stone, who was then relatively unknown.

But Amanda has also struggled with mental health and substance abuse problems, including Adderall addiction.

She was in a conservatorship between 2013 and 2022, before being placed in a 72-hour psychiatric hold in March 2023 - shortly before she had been due to appear at an All That reunion.

Nick Cannon

Nick Cannon started his career as a teenager on All That before becoming a rapper.

He released a single, Gigolo, with R. Kelly in 2003, as well as an anti-abortion track, Can I Live?, in 2005.

From 2009 to 2016, Nick hosted America's Got Talent, before playing Marcus in Brooklyn Nine-Nine between 2014 and 2015.

Nick was fired by ViacomCBS after making racist and anti-Semitic remarks in a 2020 podcast.

He was nominated for the Worst Supporting Actor Golden Raspberry in 2022 following his appearance in The Misfits.

Kenan Thompson

After starring in the original run of All That, Kenan Thompson became the longest-tenured cast member in the history of Saturday Night Live.

He was been nominated for five Emmys for his work on SNL, winning once.

SNL head writer Brian H Tucker said: "Put him in your sketch somewhere, anywhere, and your sketch will get better.

"Because Kenan knows how to take ordinary lines and make them funny, and take funny lines and make them special."

He went on to star in Kenan & Kel with his co-star Kel Mitchell from 1996-2000.

Thompson has also had roles in Snakes On A Plane and a 2011 cross-over episode of sitcoms iCarly and Victorious.

Kenan hosted the 74th Emmy Awards in September 2022.

He married model Christina Evangeline in 2011 before filing for divorce in 2022.

Josh Server

Josh Server was the only All That cast member to stay on the show through all six original seasons.

He later made guest appearances on Oh Yeah! Cartoons and 100 Deeds For Eddie McDowd, as well as Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide and Sam&Cat.

In 2022 Josh appeared in the Nickelodeon buddy comedy Warped!

Kel Mitchell

Kel Mitchell auditioned for Saturday Night Live in 2003, but lost out to his All That and Kenan & Kel co-star Kenan Thompson.

He was the subject of a MySpace death hoax in 2006.

The actor played T-Bone in Clifford The Big Red Dog, and appeared as a valet in the music video for Kanye West's 2004 song All Falls Down.

In 2018, Kel played BeansMcBeans in the SpongeBob SquarePants special SpongeBob's Big Birthday Blowout.

He is the author of the 2022 book Blessed Mode: 90 Days To Level Up Your Faith.

Kel married the rapper Asia Lee in 2012.

They have a daughter born in 2017 and a son born in 2020.

Lisa Foiles

Lisa Foiles went on from All That to become a gaming journalist for the website Kotaku.

She has also produced several bestselling graphic novels before appearing in The Game Station's live-action adaptation of The Legend Of Zelda.

The actress modelled for the Girls Of Geek calendar, and lives in Las Vegas.
