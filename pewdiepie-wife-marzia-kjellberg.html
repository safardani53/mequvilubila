Who is PewDiePie's wife Marzia Kjellberg?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 23, 2024

YOUTUBER PewDiePie has hundreds of millions of followers around the world.

But his wife Marzia Kjellberg is also a force to be reckoned with, when it comes to the world of social media.

Who is Marzia Kjellberg?

Marzia Kjellberg was born on October 21, 1992, in Arzignano, located in the Province of Vicenza, Italy.

She is an Italian businesswoman and influencer.

Marzia got together with PewDiePie, whose real name is Felix Arvid Ulf Kjellberg, in 2011 after meeting over email.

Together with her husband, and their beloved dogs. she moved to Japan in 2022 from the UK.

What is Marzia Kjellberg known for?

Like her famous husband, Marzia is also a social media star.

She was previously well known for her YouTube channel, which was named after herself.

However, in 2018 Marzia announced her retirement from vlogging on the streaming site.

At the time she uploaded a video to her channel explaining her decision.

Marzia revealed she wanted to focus on her own website, as the negatively that YouTube can sometimes bring had "affected her mental health".

She ended her video explaining that she was ready for "something new in [her] life."

After she uploaded her final vlog, she decided to remove her videos stating, "Having videos up, after making this decision, would have seemed like taking a step back."

Meanwhile, Marzia's previous success on her YouTube channel allowed her to start designing her own clothing.

Since 2015 she has launched several fashion lines including nail polish, home decor products, and most notably clothing.

Marzia is known for her "Daisy" shoes, and also her perfume called Lemon.

She also has a very popular Instagram handle, and as of July 2023 has over 8M followers on her page @itsmarziapie.

When did Marzia Kjellberg and PewDiePie get married?

The couple got hitched in 2019 after they started dating in 2011.

After their wedding Marzia shared on Instagram: "Yesterday, the 19th of August - after exactly 8 years since we met - we celebrated our wedding with our closest friends and family.

"It was the most beautiful day, which I will treasure forever. I am so thankful for all the people that made time to attend the event and helped us celebrate our marriage.

"I feel like I'm the luckiest person and I'm so full of love. Incredibly ecstatic to be able to call Felix my husband for the rest of our lives."

Do they have any children?

In July 2023 the couple revealed they had welcomed their first child together, a son called Björn.

PewDiePie captioned the post: "11/ 07/ 2023. Welcome to the family, little Björn."
