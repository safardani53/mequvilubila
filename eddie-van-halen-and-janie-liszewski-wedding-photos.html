Eddie Van Halen and Janie Liszewski Wedding (PHOTOS)

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Eddie Van Halen and his girlfriend of three years, Janie Liszewski, 39, exchanged wedding vows at a 7:00 p.m. garden ceremony on Saturday night in Studio City, California. The bride is an actress, stuntwoman and has served as Eddie’s publicist since 2007.

The 54-year-old groom’s best man was his 18-year-old son Wolfgang. The ceremony was officiated by Eddie’s brother, ordained minister Alex Van Halen.

Eddie and son Wolfie were dressed in Sy Devore cotton pants, untucked shirts and flip-flop sandals. Attending the casual nuptials were 100 guests, including Van Halen’s first wife Valerie Bertinelli – who recently rocked a bikini. The couple divorced in 2006 after 25 years of marriage.

The wedding was a non-alcoholic affair. The newlyweds plan to honeymoon in New York, Germany and the Netherlands.
