Who left I'm a Celebrity last night?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, March 18, 2024

TONIGHT, one campmate will be crowned the 2022 King or Queen of the jungle.

One more I'm A Celebrity 2022 star was told they were heading home after hosts Ant and Dec delivered the news in camp last night (November 26, 2022).

Who left I'm a Celebrity last night?

The celebs dressed up in colourful outfits - finished with matching capes for the Celebrity Cyclone, the legendary and slippery I'm A Celebrity obstacle course and won four stars for camp.

But after the frolics were over, Ant & Dec later joined camp to reveal rugby star Mike Tindall was the latest star to be leaving camp meaning Jill Scott, Owen Warner and Matt Hancock have all made it through to tonight's grand final.

When is the I'm A Celebrity final?

ITV confirmed that the I'm A Celebrity final will air on Sunday, November 27, 2022, at 9pm.
