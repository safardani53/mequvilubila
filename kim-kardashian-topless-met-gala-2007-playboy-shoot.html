Almost immediately after photos of Kim's ensemble were shared on Twitter, fans began drawing comparisons to her 2007 Playboy look.

For the spread, the Skims mogul posed completely nude with long strands of pearls draped all around her body.

She wore strappy heels, a pair of earrings, and her long flowing black hair hanging down her back as she struck a series of sexy poses.

FAMILY AFFAIR

Kim was not along, however, as her daughter North West joined her in New York City for the event.

Photos snapped before Kim made her debut at the Met Gala showed her strolling out of her hotel with her eldest child by her side.

North, nine, had on a chic tweed blazer over a white top which she accessorized with a pearl necklace not dissimilar to the ones adorning her mom's look.

She kept it casual on the bottom, rocking baggy jeans and chunky black boots.

Her hair was styled in a sleek half-up, half-down style.

She also appeared to be carrying a purse that looked like a massive pearl.

Kim was joined by sisters Kylie and Kendall Jenner for an interview as she made her way inside the event.

Kim also spoke solo with Variety about her outfit, admitting that it wasn't easy to walk in.

"It's always hard to walk at a Met. But I don't really care about comfort. I care about the vibe and what it feels like and what it looks like," she explained.

As for why she opted for this look, she did not confirm that it was a nod to her Playboy photos but did say: "I just felt like being a little bit more glamorous this year, like pearls."
