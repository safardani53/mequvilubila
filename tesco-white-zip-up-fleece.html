Tesco fans go wild over its fluffy half-zip up fleece - it's cosy and will keep you toasty this wint

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, August 23, 2024

WITH temperatures dropping, it's time to replenish your wardrobe with winter essentials.

If you're still looking for new season must-haves that will keep you toasty without breaking the budget, best get heading to Tesco, as fans keep praising a fluffy half-zip up fleece.

One of those obsessed with the F&F latest range is model Aaliyah Allwood, from the UK, who took to TikTok to show off her latest finds - and we're certain you'll love them just as much.

First up in the now-viral video, the stunner demonstrated, was, of course, the star of the shopping haul - a cosy fluffy teddy fleece in cream.

The oversized fleece, which retails for £29, is perfect for the colder mornings, lounging around the house or running errands, and is super easy to style too.

In the clip, Alliyah had chosen to wear it with a pair of nude leggings, the trendy mini Uggs, as well as a sleek bun.

Ann-Marie said: "Maybe I'd prefer it in the black, I think the pink might just be a bit too much, but it's a cute dress."

Next she showed of a oversized jumper for £19 in an "oat colour" which looked so cosy.

"It's a nice cosy jumper hat you could dress up a little bit because there's a cross over and wrap at the front," the fashionista explained.

Keeping with the comfy theme, Ann-Marie showed of a pair of black joggers, perfect for curling up on the sofa.

She loved the fit on the joggers too and was amazed by how well they fit her waist without being too tight or gaping at the back.

She also tried on a bright orange jumper that would help you stand out from the crowd, "this was the first thing I put in my basket, I do love it," she said.

But that wasn't the only jumper she grabbed, Ann-Marie also bought a chic white one for £17.50.

She said: "It's a little bit pricey but it's good quality and will wash well, Tesco's clothes are very nice."
