Toby Hemingway: Dating Taylor Swift?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, April 2, 2024

In a Web chat this week, Taylor Swift said she has a “tendency to run from love.”

But according to a rumor, the artist is fighting this tendency and giving a new man a chance.

Sources have linked Swift to Toby Hemingway, the actor who makes out with her in the music video for “A Rose to the Fallen.” Who the heck is Hemingway?

He’s 27 years old and he’s British. Major television appearance have included guest-starring roles on CSI: Miami and Bones. He also played a role in the 2006 movie The Covenant.

As she admits herself, Swift has never been part of a successful, long-term relationship. While she’s reportedly given Taylor Lautner and Cory Monteith a chance over the last year, neither worked out.

Here’s wishing good luck to Toby and Taylor! If they really are together, we hope it goes well.
