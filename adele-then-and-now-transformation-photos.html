See Photos Of Her Transformation Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, March 1, 2024

Celebrate Adele’s birthday with a look at her glamour evolution over the years, from when she first came on the scene in 2008, all the way to becoming a Grammy-winning artist!

Adele has truly transformed since bursting onto the music scene in 2006. Going from a low-key singer-songwriter to an all-around music diva, it’s no wonder her style has shifted over the years. And it’s not just her duds that have evolved. From showing off long locks and bangs with dark-colored outfits in 2006-2008 to stepping on stage with lighter strands and no bangs to cutting her hair into a classic bob in 2011, this talented lady sure knows how to leave a lasting impression!

Whether we’re admiring her in an elegant gown as she’s accepting her armfuls of Grammys or cheering her on as she poses in casual sweaters for a gorgeous photoshoot, Adele always hits the mark.
