Pregnant Rihannas Bump Pics Ahead of Welcoming Baby No. 2

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, March 30, 2024

Preparing to be a mother of two! After wowing the world with her maternity fashion before welcoming her baby boy, pregnant Rihanna is back to styling a baby bump to perfection.

The Fenty Beauty founder announced at the Super Bowl on February 12 that she and boyfriend ASAP Rocky are expecting their second child. Rihanna performed a 13-minute mashup of her greatest hits for the halftime show with her bump on display in an all-red look with two different oversized coats.

“They wanted another baby and were trying, but the pregnancy came sooner than expected!” a source told Us at the time.

According to the insider, the two musicians were “surprised the second pregnancy happened so fast” but are “so thrilled to be parents again.”

The source added: “They love their family and are so happy to be adding another member.”

Us confirmed in May 2022 that Rihanna and Rocky welcomed their first child, a son whose name they haven’t publicly shared.

“Rihanna and ASAP have only shared the baby’s name with close family members,” a second source said. “They are keeping it a secret until they’re ready to share it with the world.”

The “Don’t Stop the Music” songstress had fully embraced motherhood, per the insider.

“They have some help, but Rihanna has always said she wants to be hands-on. She’s a very caring person … very nurturing,” the source explained at the time, adding that “she won’t want to leave her baby’s side” as she navigated her various businesses.

Us exclusively revealed in February 2020 that Rihanna and Rocky were hooking up. They didn’t confirm their romance until more than a year later, with the rapper calling her the “love of my life” and “The One” during an interview with GQ.

“People don’t get out of the friend zone very easily with me,” Rihanna later shared in an interview of her own with Vogue in May 2022. “And I certainly took a while to get over how much I know him and how much he knows me because we also know how much trouble we can land each other in.”
