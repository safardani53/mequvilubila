The Makanai: Cooking for the Maiko House Is Set To Released On Netflix

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, October 1, 2024

The Makanai: Cooking for the Maiko House, a new Netflix series from acclaimed Japanese director Hirokazu Kore-eda, is due to launch on the site on Thursday, January 12, 2023, at 3 a.m. ET. It follows the narrative of a young girl who comes to Kyoto to pursue her ambition of becoming a Makanai, or traditional chef. The series is based on Aiko Koyama’s manga and stars Mori Nana in the main role, as well as a number of others in key supporting parts. Hirokazu Kore-eda is best known for his award-winning film Shoplifters.

Netflix’s The Makanai: Cooking for the Maiko House: Trailer, Plot, What to Expect, and More Information

On December 5, 2022, Netflix released the official trailer for The Makanai: Cooking for the Maiko House, which gives a glimpse into an arcane world with interesting people. The trailer begins with a voiceover saying,

“It’s as though we’ve gone back in time. So here is where we will live.”

Following that, the trailer follows the lives of the two main protagonists, who have just relocated to Kyoto to pursue their ambitions of becoming Makanai. The two girls are seen learning to make traditional meals and pursuing various art forms while mingling with the many residents of the residence.

Overall, the teaser has a heartfelt tone that fans of the director’s prior work will definitely like. Along with the teaser, Netflix has released a brief synopsis of the series, which reads:

“Set in Kyoto’s geisha district, this series follows Sumire, an apprentice geisha, and Kiyo, a chef at the maiko home where they dwell, and depicts their everyday lives via wonderful cuisine.”

The description goes on to say:

“Showrunner Hirokazu Kore-eda, producer Genki Kawamura, and a star-studded ensemble that includes Nana Mori and Natsuki Deguchi collaborate on this drama adaptation of the mega-hit comic that has sold over 2.7 million copies!”

According to the description and trailer, audiences can expect a warm, emotional drama that addresses a variety of intriguing subjects such as tradition, dreams, and the beauty of human connections, among others. The Makanai: Cooking for the Maiko House will allegedly include nine episodes, all of which will air on the same day, January 12, 2023.

A short peek at the cast and crew of The Makanai: Cooking for the Maiko House.

Natsuki Deguchi and Nana Mori star in the film Makanai: Cooking for the Maiko House. Both look terrific in the clip, effortlessly capturing their characters’ innocence and charm. Natsuki has been in Kotaro Lives Alone and Girl Gun Lady, while Nana has appeared in The Forbidden Magic, A Warmed Up Love, and many more films. Alongside them are performers such as Ai Hashimoto, Keiko Matsuzaka, and many more who play important supporting parts.

Hirokazu Kore-eda, the show’s director, has directed some of the most celebrated films of the twenty-first century, including Still Walking, Nobody Knows, and Shoplifters, to mention a few. On Thursday, January 12, 2023, don’t forget to watch The Makanai: Cooking for the Maiko House on Netflix.
