Hearts vs Rangers live stream FREE: What TV channel, team news, and kick-off time for huge Scottish

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, January 31, 2024

RANGERS head to Edinburgh with Steven Gerrard knowing that a win will put them top of the Scottish Premiership for the first time since he took charge.

The Gers impressed with ten men in a 0-0 draw against Villarreal and will now aim to move above Celtic, who are busy with the Betfred Cup final against Aberdeen this weekend.

When is Hearts vs Rangers?

Hearts vs Rangers will take place on Sunday, December 2.

It's an early start for Gers fans with the match set to kick off at 12pm.

The match will be played at Tynecastle, Edinburgh.

Is Hearts vs Rangers on TV and can I live stream it?

Yes. Hearts vs Rangers will be live on BT Sport 1 with coverage commencing at 11.30am.

To live stream the match, go to the BT Sport website or the BT Sport App.

You can also follow all the action on the SunSport LIVE BLOG.
