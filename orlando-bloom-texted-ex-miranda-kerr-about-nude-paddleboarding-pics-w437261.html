Orlando Bloom Texted Ex Miranda Kerr About Nude Paddleboarding Pics

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 24, 2024

This exchange, though! Orlando Bloom‘s bulge may have gone public, but the actor privately contacted his ex-wife, Miranda Kerr, to warn her about his nude paddleboarding photos. The former Victoria’s Secret model, 33, revealed the juicy tidbit during KIIS FM’s Kyle & Jackie O on Tuesday, August 30. Hear what she said about the controversial pictures in the video above!

“I don’t know if you’ve seen the same photos. But even when people are paddleboarding, people are supposed to be wearing pants of some description,” cohost Kyle said.

“Oh, my God, he texted me,” Kerr jumped in. “And he was like, ‘Um, I’m really embarrassed. Some photos are coming out. Just thought I should let you know.’ And I was like, ‘Hmm … right, what were you thinking? Seriously, what were you thinking?'”

The Pirates of the Caribbean actor and Kerr split in 2013 after three years of marriage and are parents of son Flynn, 5.

Earlier this month, Bloom, 39, stripped down to nothing while vacationing with girlfriend Katy Perry on a beach in Sardinia, Italy. The U.K.’s Daily Star ran the revealing pics on its cover with an embarrassed face emoji covering his package.

Bloom may have been embarrassed, but he also laughed it off. “[Orlando] finds the whole thing amusing,” an insider told Us Weekly at the time. As for Perry, the source added that the situation wasn’t “something that would annoy her.”

As previously reported, Bloom and the “Roar” singer, 31, began dating after hitting it off at the 2016 Golden Globes in January. Kerr moved on as well and got engaged to Snapchat cofounder and CEO Evan Spiegel last month.
