Dani Behr Age, Biography

Dani Behr was born in Mill Hill, London, England on July 9, 1970. She is of mixed ethnicity and British nationality. In 2023, she celebrated her 53rd birthday. Her father is one half of the real estate firm Behr & Butchoff, and her mother is an interior designer. She attended the Sylvia Young Theatre School and was a member of the girl group Faith, Hope, and Charity.

Initially discovered by Pet Shop Boys manager Tom Watkins, she was cast in his female Bros concept, Faith Hope & Charity, alongside Diana Barrand and future Xpansions member Sally Ann Marsh, and signed to the WEA record label. However, the trio failed, and Behr went into television production.
