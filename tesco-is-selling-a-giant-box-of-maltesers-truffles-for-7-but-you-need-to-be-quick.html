Tesco is selling a GIANT box of Maltesers Truffles for 7 - but you need to be quick

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, February 5, 2024

CHOCOLATE fans, listen up: Tesco is selling a giant box of Maltesers Truffles for £7.

The sweet treat, perfect for enjoying with the family this Christmas, was spotted by Instagram user, Insta Foodie Twins.

At 336g, you get just over six times the amount of chocolate compared to the small 54g box for £1.50.

The medium 200g box is currently on offer for £3.50 – it’s usually £5.

A photograph of the truffles caused much excitement on social media.

One user wrote: "I know what I'm treating myself to, Maltesers are life [sic]."

Another fan of the truffles said the box simply wasn't big enough, writing: "Not big enough they're amazing, make it giant [sic]."

The huge box, which is expected to fly off the shelves, comes in at 1898 calories so we’d recommend sharing.

The Malteser Truffles range launched to much fanfare earlier this year with the boxes coming in three sizes - a 54g Token Gift Box, a 200g Medium Gift Box and a 336g Large Gift Box.

Malteser Luxury Chocolates are also now a thing - featuring 16 giant chocolate bites individually wrapped in a red and gold polka dot foil.

Last week we reported that Tesco is now selling BUCKETS of Maltesers for just £4.
