McDonald's app goes down as customers are not able to place their orders

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, March 27, 2024

MCDONALD'S is having problems with its digital service and users are unable to order their favorites.

There have been more than 2,600 reports of outages through the fast food chain's platforms, as of 3.41pm ET, according to Downdetector.

The vast majority of the issues are with the app, with 52 percent of the reported problems coming from there.

When it comes to logging in, 35 percent of the issues are coming from there.

The remaining 10 percent of problems come McDonald's website.

And fans are frustrated, with one stating on Twitter that the "Mcdonald's app is f**king broke. Can't sign in. Can't order."

The U.S. Sun tried to log in to the app with an Apple ID and got an internal service error message.

The U.S. Sun has reached out to the company for comment.

MORE MCDONALD'S NEWS

The frustration comes after McDonald's has been making major changes to its menu.

On Tuesday, it launched a drink to honor the purple puppet Grimace.

It's a shake that's made of vanilla soft serve and berry flavors with whipped cream as the topping.

Also, McDonald's recently brought back a few freeze treats, which are as follows:

  • Frozen Coca-Cola
  • Frozen Fanta Blue Raspberry
  • Frozen Sprite Lymonade

Additionally, McDonald's has debuted a U.S. version of its Strawberry Shortcake McFlurry on its national menu.

It is vanilla soft served surrounded by "strawberry-flavored clusters and crispy, buttery shortbread cookies," according to the company.

But for the time being, fans of the fast food chain might not be able to order any of these new items through the mobile app.

You may do so in-store, however.

Thankfully, McDonald's store locator tool is still up and running.
