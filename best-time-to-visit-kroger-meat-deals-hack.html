I'm a Kroger shopper - the exact time you should go to the store to get amazing deals on steaks and

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, May 15, 2024

A TIKTOKER has revealed the prime time to visit the grocery store to get the best deals on meat.

Bo, known as @bothegoat on TikTok, impressed viewers with this scheduling trick that his followers agree is a hack for the best meat prices.

"Kroger cheat code, one take...Between the hours of 7 and 8am, the butchers take meat that is about to expire, and put it out," he said.

"Look at the marbling on this bad baby right here," he gloated, showing off a Texas Angus steak from Nolan Ryan Beef

It had been reduced to just $9.07 for quick sale during his morning visit.

"Get some skirt steak for the low, ground beef for the low," his voice says as the video pans over the variety of price-reduced meats.

"Save a couple dollars, get to Kroger early in the morning," Bo closed the video.

The video's caption goes on to describe all the meaty possibilities: "Ribeyes, beef, steaks, burgers, endless amounts of finds!"

One follower said in agreement, "Been doing this for years. It's a great deal. All you gotta do is cook it soon or freeze it same day."

A TikTok user with a large family who benefits from the discounts confirmed Bo's tactic in the comments section.
