Poundland fans spot a new 1 perfume they reckon is a dupe for the posh Joop Homme - but it's over

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 19, 2024

SHOPPERS have found a Poundland perfume they think is a close match for the designer Joop Homme scent.

Although we love luxury scents, what we don't love as much is the hefty price tag they often come with - but luckily, discount store Poundland has come to rescue once again with yet another fantastic dupe.

According to cash-savvy beauty lovers, Poundland's new  Mighty Pour Homme Eau De Toilette smells "just like" Joop Homme and it can be purchased in a 100ml bottle.

The popular retailer is also selling another version, Him Pour Homme, which comes in a chic burgundy packaging - and both aromas have already become a hit amongst money-saving shoppers.

The high-end scent, which sells for an eye-watering £65 on the high street, boasts notes of orange blossom, cinnamon and amber, and is said to be a "timeless statement of individuality and masculine sensuality", according to The Perfume Shop.

However, if you can justify spending a fortune on some perfume, then Poundland's version is thought to be just as good - and it sells for a fraction of the price as well.

Unlike the real deal, the discount dupe retails for a quid, saving customers over £60 - cha-ching.

One Facebook user, Dan B Drake, posted a snap of the Poundland finds products on the popular group Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK and wrote: "Who has tried these? They smells just like Joop & Paco Rabanne.

''Just £1 each in Poundland,'' the shopper advised others to not sleep on the discount deal in his post.

Hundreds of others raced to the comment section, where one said: ''Home Bargains sell this kind of stuff. Smell loads better and last all day.''

A mum chimed in: "My 6 year old little boy wanted some aftershave so I brought him these, they're not something I would buy for an adult, however my 6 year old loves the fact he's got perfume as he calls it."

Someone else penned: "These kind of sprays are perfect for everyday/ work/ on a budget. Some people haven’t got £120 to spend on aftershave or others want to save anything expensive for special occasions."

"I think they smell great,'' another member of the popular page added.

However, there were also quite a few of those who weren't raving about the £1 buy, warning others the aroma didn't last long.

"They may smell like them but they will last about 5 minutes. You’d have to spray it on you constantly all night,'' a Poundland shopper claimed.

 "They do but smell goes straight away,'' a second agreed.

Looking to replenish your perfume collection on the cheap?

Then alternatively, you can find Jo Malone dupes in Marks & Spencer - and they're £105 cheaper.
