Mother's Day 2023: See How the Stars Celebrated in Photos

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

A very Happy Mother’s Day to the best mamas! Priyanka Chopra Jonas, Heather Rae El Moussa (née Young) and more celebrity moms are feeling all the love on Sunday, May 14.

“I’m so lucky to have always known the love of a mother. My mother is the strongest woman I know. And her mother was too. I come from a lineage of women who are warriors and I was blessed to be raised by many of them,” the Citadel actress, 40, gushed via Instagram on Sunday morning. “My Mum, my aunts, my grandmothers. Thank you Ma, you’re the greatest gift in my life. I could not be more grateful you’re mine!”

Chopra Jonas — who shares 16-month-old daughter Malti Marie with husband Nick Jonas — uploaded sweet generational photos of herself, Malti and the little girl’s grandmothers. In one snap, the mother-daughter pair snuggled with the Quantico alum’s mom, Madhu Chopra. In a second, they were joined by the 30-year-old Jonas Brothers’ musician’s mother, Denise Jonas, at a meal.

“As a new mum, I have so much respect for all the providers and nurtures [sic], (my mother insists mothers are also providers, I agree😍), who devote themselves to the next generation. My gratitude,” Chopra Jonas added. “Also thank you Denise for raising an exceptional son and for all the love you bestow upon our family. I’m so blessed.”

She continued: “And I love you, Malti Marie. Thank you for making me a mama. It’s the greatest honor of my life that you chose me. ❤️🙏🏽.”

The 35-year-old Selling Sunset personality, for her part, marked her first Mother’s Day since welcoming baby boy Tristan Jay earlier this year.

“Mother’s Day came early. Thanks for the beautiful flowers my loves,” Heather gushed via Instagram Story on Saturday, May 13, tagging husband Tarek El Moussa and kids Taylor, 12, Brayden, 7, and Tristan, 4 months.

Tarek, 41, was previously married to Christina Hall (née Haack), with whom he coparents Taylor and Brayden. After marrying Heather in October 2021, they later welcomed their first child together in January.

Hilarie Burton Morgan was also showered with love and appreciation on the Sunday holiday.

“You’re sweet [Jeffrey Dean Morgan]! 🌹🌈🌹🌈🌹🌈🌹🌈🌹🌈🌹,” the One Tree Hill alum, 40, wrote via Instagram after her husband, 57, surprised her with a bouquet of roses.
