Jenna Jameson: Why I Removed My Breast Implants

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, April 21, 2024

Adult film star Jenna Jameson has decided to retire from adult filmmaking, and that's not all she's left behind.

On August 1, Jameson visited Beverly Hills plastic surgeon Garth Fisher to have her breast implants removed. Though she continues to run her $30 million-a-year ClubJenna empire, the 33-year-old is focusing on a Hollywood biopic, a comic book and a clothing line, due out next year. Nearly two weeks after the three-hour surgery that took the 5-foot-6 star from a D-cup to a C-up, she sat down with Us Weekly to discuss her new body, her boyfriend (ultimate fighter Tito Ortiz, 33) and the future.

On how removing the implants changed her: "Even for women with naturally large boobs, getting a reduction is so freeing. I feel like I can stand up straighter. Before, when I jogged, I had to hold my boobs. I looked like I was molesting myself!"

On how she felt postsurgery: "Ecstatic. The first thing I did when I got home was open my bra. I wasn't supposed to but I did. I was so happy, I cried. It was like looking into the mirror when I was 17."

On whether she's done with porn forever: "Yes. A hundred percent."

On who will play her in a movie about her life: "I would love Scarlett Johansson to play me. I think Rachel McAdams is amazing, and Sienna Miller."
